§501. Definition

In this chapter “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

(Added Pub. L. 90–235, §2(a)(1)(B), Jan. 2, 1968, 81 Stat. 753.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 501 was renumbered 502 of this title.
