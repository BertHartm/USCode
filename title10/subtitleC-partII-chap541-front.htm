Repeal of Chapter

Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, §§1629(b)(1), 1691(b)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2963, 3026, provided that, effective Oct. 1, 1996, this chapter is repealed.
