[§6975. Repealed. Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title V, §533(b), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 315; Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title X, §1073(d)(1)(C), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1905]

Section, added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title V, §556(b)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2774, related to position of athletic director of Naval Academy and to administration of nonappropriated fund account for athletics program of Naval Academy.

Effective Date of Repeal

Section 1073(d)(1)(C) of Pub. L. 105–85 provided that the amendment made by that section is effective as of Feb. 10, 1996, and as if included in the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1996, Pub. L. 104–106, as enacted.

Pub. L. 104–201, div. A, title X, §1074(c)(1), Sept. 23, 1996, 110 Stat. 2660, provided that: “Section 533(b) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1996 (Public Law 104–106; 110 Stat. 315) [repealing section 556(b)(1) of Pub. L. 103–337 which added this section and repealing provisions set out as notes under this section] shall apply as if enacted as of December 31, 1995.”

Section 533(b) of Pub. L. 104–106, as amended by Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title X, §1073(d)(1)(C), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1905, provided that the repeal by that section of section 556(b) and (e) of Pub. L. 103–337, which added this section and enacted provisions set out as notes under this section, is effective as of Oct. 5, 1994.

Effective Date

Section 556(e) of Pub. L. 103–337, which provided that this section, as added by section 556(b) of Pub. L. 103–337, was to take effect on Jan. 1, 1996, was repealed by Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title V, §533(b), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 315; Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title X, §1073(d)(1)(C), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1905.

Time of Establishment of Nonappropriated Fund Account

Section 556(b)(3) of Pub. L. 103–337, which provided that the account referred to in former subsec. (b) of this section was to be established not later than the effective date set forth in section 556(e) of Pub. L. 103–337 [formerly set out as a note above], was repealed by Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title V, §533(b), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 315; Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title X, §1073(d)(1)(C), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1905.
