§10508. Definition

In this chapter, the term “State” includes the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and Guam and the Virgin Islands.

(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title IX, §904(a), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2827.)
