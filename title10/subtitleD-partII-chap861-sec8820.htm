§8820. Air National Guard of United States officers: discharge

(a) The Secretary may terminate the reserve appointment of an officer of the Air National Guard of the United States and withdraw his Federal recognition as an officer of the Air National Guard, if he has been absent without leave for three months.

(b) An officer of the Air National Guard of the United States whose Federal recognition as an officer of the Air National Guard is withdrawn under section 323(b) of title 32 shall be discharged from his reserve appointment.

(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 546.)

Repeal of Section

Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, §§1629(c)(2), 1691(b)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2963, 3026, provided that, effective Oct. 1, 1996, this section is repealed.
