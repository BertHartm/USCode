§133a. Principal Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics

(a) There is a Principal Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics, appointed from civilian life by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.

(b) The Principal Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics shall assist the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics in the performance of the Under Secretary's duties.
