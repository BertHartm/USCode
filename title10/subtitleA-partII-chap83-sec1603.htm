§1603. Limit on pay

Nothing in sections 1601 and 1602 of this title shall be construed to allow the aggregate amount payable to a member of the Defense Intelligence Senior Executive Service under those sections during any fiscal year to exceed the annual rate payable for positions at level I of the Executive Schedule in effect at the end of such year.

(Added Pub. L. 97–89, title VII, §701(a)(1), Dec. 4, 1981, 95 Stat. 1160; amended Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII, §1302(a)(3), Nov. 8, 1985, 99 Stat. 738; Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title XIII, §1343(a)(9), Nov. 14, 1986, 100 Stat. 3992.)

References in Text

Level I of the Executive Schedule, referred to in text, is set out in section 5312 of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

Amendments

1986—Pub. L. 99–661 struck out “this” after “Nothing in” and substituted “those sections” for “this sections 1601 and 1602 of this title”.

1985—Pub. L. 99–145 substituted “sections 1601 and 1602 of this title” for “chapter” in two places.
