§3393. Commissioned officers: sea or foreign service not to be required for promotion

Sea or foreign service may not be made a condition to the promotion of reserve commissioned officers in reserve grades.

(Added Pub. L. 85–861, §1(80)(E), Sept. 2, 1958, 72 Stat. 1479.)

Repeal of Section

Section repealed effective Oct. 1, 1996, see note set out preceding section 3351 of this title.
