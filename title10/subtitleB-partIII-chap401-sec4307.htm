§4307. Director of civilian marksmanship: detail

The President may detail a commissioned officer of the Army or of the Marine Corps as director of civilian marksmanship, to serve under the direction of the Secretary of the Army.

(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 235.)

Historical and Revision Notes
Revised sectionSource (U.S. Code)Source (Statutes at Large)
4307 32:182. Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 418 (11th proviso under “National Guard”), 39 Stat. 648.

The word “detail” is substituted for the word “appoint”, since the filling of the office is not appointment to an office in the constitutional sense. The words “in his discretion” are omitted as surplusage. The word “commissioned” is inserted, since the source statute historically applied only to commissioned officers.
