[§4345. Repealed. Pub. L. 98–94, title X, §1004(a)(2), Sept. 24, 1983, 97 Stat. 658]

Section, act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 242, related to selection of Filipinos for instruction at the Military Academy. See section 4344.

Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective one year after Sept. 24, 1983, see section 1004(d) of Pub. L. 98–94, set out as an Effective Date of 1983 Amendment note under section 4344 of this title.
