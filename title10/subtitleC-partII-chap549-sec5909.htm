§5909. Sea or foreign service not required

Sea or foreign service may not be made a prerequisite for the promotion of reserve officers under this chapter.

(Added Pub. L. 85–861, §1(133), Sept. 2, 1958, 72 Stat. 1506.)

Repeal of Section

Section repealed effective Oct. 1, 1996, see note set out preceding section 5891 of this title.
