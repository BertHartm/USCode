§2885. Expiration of authority

The authority to enter into a contract under this subchapter shall expire five years after the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1996.

(Added Pub. L. 104–106, div. B, title XXVIII, §2801(a)(1), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 551.)

References in Text

The date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1996, referred to in text, is the date of enactment of Pub. L. 104–106, which was approved Feb. 10, 1996.
