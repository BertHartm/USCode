§2885. Expiration of authority

The authority to enter into a contract under this subchapter shall expire on February 10, 2001.

(Added Pub. L. 104–106, div. B, title XXVIII, §2801(a)(1), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 551; amended Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title X, §1073(a)(61), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1903.)

Amendments

1997—Pub. L. 105–85 substituted “on February 10, 2001” for “five years after the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1996”.
