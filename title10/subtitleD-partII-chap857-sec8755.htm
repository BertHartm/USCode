§8755. Medal of honor: presentation of Medal of Honor Flag

The President shall provide for the presentation of the Medal of Honor Flag designated under section 903 of title 36 to each person to whom a medal of honor is awarded under section 8741 of this title after October 23, 2002. Presentation of the flag shall be made at the same time as the presentation of the medal under section 8741 or 8752(a) of this title.

(Added Pub. L. 107–248, title VIII, §8143(c)(3)(A), Oct. 23, 2002, 116 Stat. 1570; amended Pub. L. 107–314, div. A, title X, §1062(a)(16), Dec. 2, 2002, 116 Stat. 2650.)

Codification

Another section 8755 was renumbered section 8756 of this title.

Amendments

2002—Pub. L. 107–314 substituted “October 23, 2002” for “the date of the enactment of this section”.
