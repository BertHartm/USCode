§6257.1 Medal of honor: presentation of Medal of Honor Flag

The President shall provide for the presentation of the Medal of Honor Flag designated under section 903 of title 36 to each person to whom a medal of honor is awarded under section 6241 of this title after October 23, 2002. Presentation of the flag shall be made at the same time as the presentation of the medal under section 6241 or 6250 of this title.

(Added Pub. L. 107–248, title VIII, §8143(c)(2)(A), Oct. 23, 2002, 116 Stat. 1570; amended Pub. L. 107–314, div. A, title X, §1062(a)(16), Dec. 2, 2002, 116 Stat. 2650.)

Amendments

2002—Pub. L. 107–314 substituted “October 23, 2002” for “the date of the enactment of this section”.
