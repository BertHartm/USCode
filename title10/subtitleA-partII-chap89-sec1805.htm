§1805. Termination of program

The selection of volunteers to participate in the program under this chapter shall terminate on September 30, 1995.

(Added Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title XIII, §1322(a)(1), Oct. 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 2553.)
