§856. Repealed. Pub. L. 103–429, §7(a)(1), Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4388

Section, Pub. L. 94–210, title VII, §708, as added Pub. L. 102–533, §4(a), Oct. 27, 1992, 106 Stat. 3516, directed Secretary to develop and report to Congress within one year after Oct. 27, 1992, on a program master plan for improvements in passenger service between Boston and New York. See section 24903 of Title 49, Transportation.

Effective Date of Repeal

Section 7(a)(1) of Pub. L. 103–429 provided that the repeal by that section is effective July 5, 1994.
