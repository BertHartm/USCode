§231v. Repealed. Pub. L. 100–504, title I, §102(e)(3), Oct. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 2517

Section, act Aug. 29, 1935, ch. 812, §23, as added Aug. 12, 1983, Pub. L. 98–76, title IV, §418, 97 Stat. 438, provided that for purposes of Inspector General Act of 1978, the Railroad Retirement Board is an “establishment” and the Chairman of such Board the “head of the establishment”. See sections 9 and 11 of Pub. L. 95–452, the Inspector General Act of 1978, set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 18, 1988, see section 113 of Pub. L. 100–504, set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under section 5 of Pub. L. 95–452 [Inspector General Act of 1978] set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
