§8169. Expiration

The authority of the Secretary to enter into enhanced-use leases under this subchapter expires on December 31, 1995.

(Added Pub. L. 102–86, title IV, §401(a), Aug. 14, 1991, 105 Stat. 422; amended Pub. L. 103–452, title I, §103(d), Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4786.)

Amendments

1994—Pub. L. 103–452 substituted “December 31, 1995” for “December 31, 1994”.
