§7618. Expiration of program

The Secretary may not furnish scholarships to new participants in the Scholarship Program after December 31, 1995.

(Added Pub. L. 100–322, title II, §216(b), May 20, 1988, 102 Stat. 525, §4318; renumbered §7618, Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, §402(b)(1), May 7, 1991, 105 Stat. 238; amended Pub. L. 102–83, §4(b)(1), (2)(E), Aug. 6, 1991, 105 Stat. 404, 405; Pub. L. 102–585, title V, §523(a), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 4959.)

Amendments

1992—Pub. L. 102–585 substituted “December 31, 1995” for “September 30, 1992”.

1991—Pub. L. 102–40 renumbered section 4318 of this title as this section.

Pub. L. 102–83 substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”.
