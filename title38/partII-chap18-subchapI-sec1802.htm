§1802. Spina bifida conditions covered

This subchapter applies with respect to all forms and manifestations of spina bifida except spina bifida occulta.

(Added Pub. L. 104–204, title IV, §421(b)(1), Sept. 26, 1996, 110 Stat. 2923; amended Pub. L. 106–419, title IV, §401(e)(1), Nov. 1, 2000, 114 Stat. 1860.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 1802 was renumbered section 3702 of this title.

Amendments

2000—Pub. L. 106–419, which directed the substitution of “this subchapter” for “this chapter”, was executed by substituting “This subchapter” for “This chapter”, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

Effective Date of 2000 Amendment

Amendment by Pub. L. 106–419 effective on the first day of the first month beginning more than one year after Nov. 1, 2000, see section 401(g) of Pub. L. 106–419, set out as an Effective Date note under section 1811 of this title.
