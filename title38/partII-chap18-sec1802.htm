§1802. Spina bifida conditions covered

This chapter applies with respect to all forms and manifestations of spina bifida except spina bifida occulta.

(Added Pub. L. 104–204, title IV, §421(b)(1), Sept. 26, 1996, 110 Stat. 2923.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 1802 was renumbered section 3702 of this title.
