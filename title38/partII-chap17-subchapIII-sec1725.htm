Codification

Prior to renumbering of sections 601 to 664 of this chapter as sections 1701 to 1764 by Pub. L. 102–83, §5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, 105 Stat. 406, section 625 of this chapter, Pub. L. 85–857, Sept. 2, 1958, 72 Stat. 1144; Pub. L. 91–24, §6(a), June 11, 1969, 83 Stat. 34, which related to arrests for crimes in hospital and domiciliary reservations, was repealed by Pub. L. 93–43, §§4(b), 10(a), June 18, 1973, 87 Stat. 79, 88, effective June 18, 1973.
