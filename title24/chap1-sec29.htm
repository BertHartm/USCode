§§29, 29a. Repealed. Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 654, §1(45), 65 Stat. 703

Section 29, acts Mar. 15, 1920, ch. 100, §1, 41 Stat. 530; Mar. 4, 1921, ch. 156, 41 Stat. 1365, related to disposal of surplus material for the Public Health Service. See section 483 of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.
