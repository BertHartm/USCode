§2468b. Identification of State-imposed requirements

Any State rule or policy imposed on the administration or operation of programs funded by this chapter, including any rule or policy based on State interpretation of any Federal law, regulation, or guideline, shall be identified as a State imposed requirement.

(Pub. L. 88 210, title V, §513, as added Pub. L. 101 392, title V, §501(c)(2), Sept. 25, 1990, 104 Stat. 833.)

Effective Date

Section effective July 1, 1991, see section 702(a) of Pub. L. 101 392, set out as an Effective Date of 1990 Amendment note under section 2301 of this title.
