§7931. Short title

This part may be cited as the “Alaska Native Educational Equity, Support and Assistance Act”.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, §9301, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3805.)
