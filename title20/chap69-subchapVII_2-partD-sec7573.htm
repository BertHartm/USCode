§7573. Notification

The State educational agency, and when applicable, the State board for postsecondary education, shall be notified within three working days of the date an award under part A of this subchapter is made to an eligible entity within the State.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, §7403, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3744.)
