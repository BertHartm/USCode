§977. Annual report to Congress

The Council shall report annually to the Congress (1) all claims actually paid pursuant to this chapter during the preceding fiscal year, (2) pending claims against the Council under this chapter as of the close of that fiscal year, and (3) the aggregate face value of contracts entered into by the Council which are outstanding at the close of that fiscal year.

(Pub. L. 94–158, §8, Dec. 20, 1975, 89 Stat. 846.)
