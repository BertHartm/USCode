§5896. Clarification regarding State standards and assessments

Notwithstanding any other provision of this subchapter, standards or State assessments described in a State improvement plan submitted in accordance with section 5886 of this title shall not be required to be certified by the Council.

(Pub. L. 103–227, title III, §316, Mar. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 184.)
