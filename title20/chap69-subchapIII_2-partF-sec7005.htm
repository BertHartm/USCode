§7005. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $200,000,000 for fiscal year 1995 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the four succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 89 10, title III, §3605, as added Pub. L. 103 382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3672.)
