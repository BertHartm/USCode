§6924. Applications

Each eligible entity desiring a grant, contract, or cooperative agreement under section 6921 or 6923 of this title shall submit an application to the Secretary at such time, in such manner, and accompanied by such information as the Secretary may reasonably require.

(Pub. L. 89 10, title III, §3304, as added Pub. L. 103 382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3665.)
