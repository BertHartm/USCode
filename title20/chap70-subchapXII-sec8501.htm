§8501. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Education Infrastructure Act of 1994”.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title XII, §12001, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3870.)
