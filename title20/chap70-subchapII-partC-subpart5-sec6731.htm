§6731. Short title

This subpart may be cited as the “Paul D. Coverdell Teacher Protection Act of 2001”.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title II, §2361, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title II, §201, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1667.)
