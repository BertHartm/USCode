§1235g. “Distance learning” defined

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term “distance learning” means the transmission of educational or instructional programming to geographically dispersed individuals and groups via telecommunications.

(Pub. L. 90–247, title IV, §478, formerly Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, §4708, as added Pub. L. 102–545, §3, Oct. 27, 1992, 106 Stat. 3589; renumbered §478 of Pub. L. 90–247, Pub. L. 103–252, title I, §121(a)(1), May 18, 1994, 108 Stat. 649.)
