§6054a. Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $25,000,000 for fiscal year 1995 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the four succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 103–227, title IX, §1012, formerly Pub. L. 89–10, title XIII, §13202, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3882; renumbered Pub. L. 103–227, title IX, §1012, Pub. L. 107–110, title X, §1022(a), (b), Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1987.)

Codification

Section 1022(b) of Pub. L. 107–110, which directed that section 13102 of part B of title XIII of Pub. L. 89–10 be renumbered as section 1012 of Pub. L. 103–227, was executed by renumbering section 13202 of part B of title XIII of Pub. L. 89–10 as section 1012 of Pub. L. 103–227, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

Section was formerly classified to section 8652 of this title prior to renumbering by Pub. L. 107–110.

Another section 1012 of Pub. L. 103–227 is classified to section 6062 of this title.
