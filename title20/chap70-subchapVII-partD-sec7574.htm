§7574. Continued eligibility

Entities receiving grants under this subchapter shall remain eligible for grants for subsequent activities which extend or expand and do not duplicate those activities supported by a previous grant under this subchapter. In considering applications for grants under this subchapter, the Secretary shall take into consideration the applicant's record of accomplishments under previous grants under this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, §7404, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3744.)
