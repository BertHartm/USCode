§963a. Omitted

Section, Pub. L. 98 146, title II, Nov. 4, 1983, 97 Stat. 949, which directed that persons serving on Museum Services Board continue until their successors are qualified for office, was omitted as superseded. See section 963(b) of this title as amended by Pub. L. 98 306. Similar provisions were contained in Pub. L. 97 394, title II, Dec. 30, 1982, 96 Stat. 1994.
