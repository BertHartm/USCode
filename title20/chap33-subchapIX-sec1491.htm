§1491. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Families of Children With Disabilities Support Act of 1994”.

(Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, §701, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title III, §315, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3937.)

Effective Date

Subchapter effective July 1, 1995, see section 3(a)(3)(A) of Pub. L. 103–382, set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under section 1411 of this title.
