§1109b. Program requirements

(a) Annual competition

In each fiscal year, the Secretary shall announce the factors to be examined in a demonstration project assisted under this subpart. Such factors may include—

(1) the magnitude of the reduction in class size to be achieved;

(2) the level of education and the subject areas in which the demonstration projects shall occur;

(3) the form of the instructional strategy to be demonstrated; and

(4) the duration of the project.

(b) Random techniques and appropriate comparison groups

Demonstration projects assisted under this subpart shall be designed to utilize randomized techniques or appropriate comparison groups, where feasible.

(Pub. L. 89–329, title V, §563, as added Pub. L. 102–325, title V, §501(a), July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 701.)
