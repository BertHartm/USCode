§1135e 8. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part, $10,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years. Not more than 3 percent of the amount appropriated for this part in any fiscal year may be used for purposes of section 1135e 5 of this title.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title X, §1069, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title X, §1003, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 783.)

Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1992, see section 2 of Pub. L. 102 325, set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment note under section 1001 of this title.
