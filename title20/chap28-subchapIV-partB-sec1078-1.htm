Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see section 4047(d) of Pub. L. 103–66, set out as an Effective Date of 1993 Amendments note under section 1078–8 of this title.

Continuing Applicability of Terms, Conditions, and Benefits of Loans

Terms, conditions, and benefits applicable with respect to loans provided under this section as it existed on the date preceding Aug. 10, 1993, to continue to apply after Aug. 10, 1993, notwithstanding repeal of this section, see section 4047(c) of Pub. L. 103–66, set out as a note under section 1078–8 of this title.
