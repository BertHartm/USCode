§7101. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Safe and Drug-Free Schools and Communities Act of 1994”.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, §4001, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3672.)
