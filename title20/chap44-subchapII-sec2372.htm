§2372. Consolidation of funds

(a) In general

An eligible agency receiving an allotment under sections 2321 and 2371 of this title may choose to consolidate all, or a portion of, funds received under section 2371 of this title with funds received under section 2321 of this title in order to carry out the activities described in the State plan submitted under section 2342 of this title.

(b) Notification requirement

Each eligible agency that chooses to consolidate funds under this section shall notify the Secretary, in the State plan submitted under section 2342 of this title, of the eligible agency's decision to consolidate funds under this section.

(c) Treatment of consolidated funds

Funds consolidated under this section shall be considered as funds allotted under section 2321 of this title and shall be distributed in accordance with section 2322 of this title.

(Pub. L. 88–210, title II, §202, as added Pub. L. 109–270, §1(b), Aug. 12, 2006, 120 Stat. 738.)
