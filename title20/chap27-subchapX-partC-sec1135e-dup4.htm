§1135e 4. Application

To receive a grant under this part, an eligible institution shall submit an application at such time, in such manner, and containing or accompanied by such information, as the Secretary may reasonably require. Each such application shall contain a description of the goals of the activities to be assisted.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title X, §1065, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title X, §1003, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 782.)

Effective Date
