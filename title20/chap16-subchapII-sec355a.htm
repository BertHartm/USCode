§355a. Grants to States for public library construction and library and information technology enhancement

The Secretary shall carry out a program of making grants to States which have had approved a basic State plan under section 351d of this title and have submitted a long-range program and submit annually appropriately updated programs under section 355c of this title for the construction and technology enhancement of public libraries.

(June 19, 1956, ch. 407, title II, §201, as added Feb. 11, 1964, Pub. L. 88–269, §7(a), 78 Stat. 13; amended July 19, 1966, Pub. L. 89–511, §6, 80 Stat. 313; Dec. 30, 1970, Pub. L. 91–600, §2(b), 84 Stat. 1668; Oct. 17, 1984, Pub. L. 98–480, title I, §103(b)(1), 98 Stat. 2237; Mar. 15, 1990, Pub. L. 101–254, §16(c)(2), (3), 104 Stat. 106.)
