§9864. Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of carrying out this subchapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $28,000,000 for fiscal year 2008, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 2 succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 110–69, title VI, §6304, Aug. 9, 2007, 121 Stat. 667.)
