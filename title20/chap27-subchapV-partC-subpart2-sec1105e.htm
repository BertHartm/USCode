§1105e. Fellowship repayment provisions

Repayment of the award shall be made to the Federal Government in the case of fraud or gross noncompliance.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title V, §536, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title V, §501(a), July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 686.)
