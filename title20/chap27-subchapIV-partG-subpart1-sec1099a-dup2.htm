§1099a 2. Federal reimbursement of State postsecondary review costs

(a) Payments

Subject to subsection (b) of this section, the Secretary shall reimburse the States for the costs of performing the functions required by agreements with the Secretary authorized under this subpart. Such costs shall include expenses for providing initial and continuing training to State personnel and other personnel in the State, including personnel at institutions of higher education subject to review, to serve the purposes of this subpart. Reimbursement shall be provided for necessary activities which supplement, but do not supplant, existing licensing or review functions conducted by the State. The Secretary shall also reimburse such entities for work performed by their subcontractors and consultants where such work has a direct relationship to the requirements of agreements with the Secretary under this subpart.

(b) Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of enabling the Secretary to make payments to States which have made agreements with the Secretary under this subpart, there is authorized to be appropriated $75,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years.
