§6973. Program authorized

The Secretary is authorized to make allotments to State educational agencies under section 6974 of this title to enable such agencies to award grants to local educational agencies for the purpose of providing equipment and materials to elementary schools to improve mathematics and science education in such schools.

(Pub. L. 89 10, title III, §3503, as added Pub. L. 103 382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3667.)
