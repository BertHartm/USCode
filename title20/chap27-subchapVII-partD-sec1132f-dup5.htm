§1132f 5. Issue of nonvoting stock and debt to public

The Corporation may issue, without limitation as to amount or restriction as to ownership, such nonvoting common, preferred, and preference stock, debt, and such other securities and obligations, in such amounts, at such times, and having such terms and conditions as may be deemed necessary or appropriate by its Board of Directors.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title VII, §756, as added Pub. L. 99 498, title VII, §701, Oct. 17, 1986, 100 Stat. 1532.)
