§6055d. Payments; Federal share; non-Federal share

(a) Payments

The Secretary shall pay to each eligible entity having an application approved under section 6055b of this title the Federal share of the cost of the activities described in the application.

(b) Federal share

For the purpose of subsection (a) of this section, the Federal share shall be 80 percent.

(c) Non-Federal share

The non-Federal share of the cost of activities described in the application submitted under section 6055b of this title may be in cash or in kind, fairly evaluated. At least 10 percent of such non-Federal share shall be from sources other than the Federal Government, or State or local government.
