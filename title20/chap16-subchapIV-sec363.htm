§363. Applications for library services to Indians

Any Indian tribe which desires to receive its allotment under section 351c(c)(1) of this title shall submit an application which contains such information as the Secretary may require by regulation.

(June 19, 1956, ch. 407, title IV, §403, as added Oct. 17, 1984, Pub. L. 98 480, title I, §114, 98 Stat. 2244.)
