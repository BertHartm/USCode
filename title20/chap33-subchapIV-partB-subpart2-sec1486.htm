§1486. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 1481 through 1485 of this title such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1998 through 2002.

(Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, §686, as added Pub. L. 105–17, title I, §101, June 4, 1997, 111 Stat. 154.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 686 of Pub. L. 91–230 was classified to section 1485 of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 105–17, title II, §203(b), June 4, 1997, 111 Stat. 157, effective July 1, 1998.

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in title 29 section 772.
