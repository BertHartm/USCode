§1491n. Construction

Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, nothing in subchapters I through VIII of this chapter shall be construed to apply to this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, §715, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title III, §315, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3955.)
