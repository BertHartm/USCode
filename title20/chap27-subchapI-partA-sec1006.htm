§1006. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years to carry out this part.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title I, §106, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title I, §101, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 461.)
