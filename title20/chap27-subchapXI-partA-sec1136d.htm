§1136d. Peer review

The Secretary shall designate a peer review panel to review applications submitted under this part and make recommendations for funding to the Secretary. In selecting the peer review panel, the Secretary may consult with other appropriate Cabinet-level officials and with non-Federal organizations, to ensure that the panel will be geographically balanced and be composed of representatives from public and private institutions of higher education, labor, business, State and local government, who have expertise in urban community service or in education.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title XI, §1105, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title XI, §1101, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 786.)
