§7104. Funding

There are authorized to be appropriated—

(1) $630,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the four succeeding fiscal years, for State grants under subpart 1 of part A of this subchapter; and

(2) $25,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the four succeeding fiscal years, for national programs under subpart 2 of part A of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 89 10, title IV, §4004, as added Pub. L. 103 382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3674.)
