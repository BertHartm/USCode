§1134d. Statement of purpose; designation

(a) Purpose

It is the purpose of this part to provide, through institutions of higher education, a program of grants to assist in making available the benefits of master's level, professional, and doctoral education programs to individuals from minority groups and women who are underrepresented in such programs.

(b) Designation

Each recipient of such an award under this part shall be known as a “Patricia Roberts Harris Graduate Fellow”.

(Pub. L. 89–329, title IX, §921, as added Pub. L. 99–498, title IX, §901(a), Oct. 17, 1986, 100 Stat. 1550; amended Pub. L. 102–325, title IX, §901, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 762.)
