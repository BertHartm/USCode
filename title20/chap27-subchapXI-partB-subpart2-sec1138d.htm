§1138d. Technical assistance and coordination contract

To the extent that funds are available therefor pursuant to section 1139 of this title, the Secretary may, directly or by way of grant, contract, or other arrangement—

(1) provide technical assistance to grant recipients under this subpart;

(2) collect and disseminate information with respect to programs assisted under this subpart; and

(3) evaluate such programs and issue reports on the results of such evaluations.

(Pub. L. 89–329, title XI, §1145, as added Pub. L. 102–325, title XI, §1101, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 791.)
