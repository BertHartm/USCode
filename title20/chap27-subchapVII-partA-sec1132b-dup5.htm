§1132b 5. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated $350,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years to carry out the provisions of this part.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title VII, §716, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title VII, §703, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 741.)
