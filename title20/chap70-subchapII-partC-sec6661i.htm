§6661i. Authorizations of appropriations; reservations from appropriations; sunset

(a) Authorizations

(1) FY 1999

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part and section 6362(c) of this title $260,000,000 for fiscal year 1999.

(2) FY 2000

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part and section 6362(c) of this title $260,000,000 for fiscal year 2000.

(b) Reservations

From each of the amounts appropriated under subsection (a) of this section for a fiscal year, the Secretary—

(1) shall reserve 1.5 percent to carry out section 6661f(a) of this title;

(2) shall reserve $5,000,000 to carry out section 6661g of this title; and

(3) shall reserve $10,000,000 to carry out section 6362(c) of this title.

(c) Sunset

Notwithstanding section 1226a(a) of this title, this part is not subject to extension under such section.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title II, §2260, as added Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, §101(f) [title VIII, §101(a)(2)], Oct. 21, 1998, 112 Stat. 2681–337, 2681–406.)

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 6362, 6661f, 6661g of this title.
