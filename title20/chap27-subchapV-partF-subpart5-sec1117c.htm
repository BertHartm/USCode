§1117c. Authorization of appropriations

(a) Training in early childhood education and violence counseling

To carry out activities described in section 1117 of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years.

(b) Early childhood staff training and professional enhancement

To carry out activities described in section 1117a of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated $10,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 89 329, title V, §599, as added Pub. L. 102 325, title V, §501(a), July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 719.)
