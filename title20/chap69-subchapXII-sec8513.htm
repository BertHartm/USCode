§8513. Authorization

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $200,000,000 for fiscal year 1995 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the four succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title XII, §12013, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3875.)
