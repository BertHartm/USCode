§1117b. Report

Each institution of higher education or State receiving a grant under this subpart shall submit to the Secretary program reports and evaluations at such times and containing such information as the Secretary may require.

(Pub. L. 89–329, title V, §598, as added Pub. L. 102–325, title V, §501(a), July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 719.)
