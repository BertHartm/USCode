§5918. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1995 through 1998 to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 103–227, title IV, §408, Mar. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 191.)
