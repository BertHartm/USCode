§1132c–5. Prohibition

No institution that receives a loan under this part shall also receive a grant under part A of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 89–329, title VII, §726, as added Pub. L. 102–325, title VII, §704, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 746; amended Pub. L. 103–208, §2(j)(17), Dec. 20, 1993, 107 Stat. 2481.)

L. 94–482, title I, §162(a)(3), Oct. 12, 1976, 90 Stat. 2156, related to academic facilities loan insurance, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by Pub. L. 96–374.
