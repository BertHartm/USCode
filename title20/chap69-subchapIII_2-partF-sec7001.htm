§7001. Program authorized

The Secretary shall award grants or make allocations in accordance with section 7002 of this title for the acquisition of school library media resources for the use of students, library media specialists, and teachers in elementary and secondary schools in accordance with this part.

(Pub. L. 89 10, title III, §3601, as added Pub. L. 103 382, title I, §101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3671.)
