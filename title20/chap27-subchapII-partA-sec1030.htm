§1030. Omitted

Codification

Section, Pub. L. 89–329, title II, §213, as added Pub. L. 99–498, title II, §203, Oct. 17, 1986, 100 Stat. 1289, which defined “full-time equivalent students”, was omitted in the general revision of this subchapter by Pub. L. 102–325.
