§7253. Short title

This subpart may be cited as the “Jacob K. Javits Gifted and Talented Students Education Act of 2001”.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title V, §5461, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title V, §501, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1826.)
