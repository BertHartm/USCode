§8297. Applicability

Sections 8271 and 8272 of this title shall not apply to this subpart.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title X, §10978, as added Pub. L. 106–554, §1(a)(1) [title IX, §901], Dec. 21, 2000, 114 Stat. 2763, 2763A–92.)
