§2004. Application (Rule 4)

Rules in this subpart apply in any condition of visibility.

(Pub. L. 96–591, §2, Dec. 24, 1980, 94 Stat. 3417.)

References in Text

Rules in this subpart, referred to in text, means sections 2004 to 2010 of this title.

Codification

Section was enacted as part of section 2 of Pub. L. 96–591 which enacted the Inland Navigational Rules which comprise this subchapter.

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 2013, 2018, 2019 of this title.
