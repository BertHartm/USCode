§701b–12. Compliance with flood plain management and insurance programs

Before construction of any project for local flood protection or any project for hurricane or storm damage reduction, the non-Federal interests shall agree to participate in and comply with applicable Federal flood plain management and flood insurance programs.

(Pub. L. 99–662, title IV, §402, Nov. 17, 1986, 100 Stat. 4133; Pub. L. 100–676, §14, Nov. 17, 1988, 102 Stat. 4026.)

Amendments

1988—Pub. L. 100–676 inserted “or any project for hurricane or storm damage reduction” after “local flood protection”.
