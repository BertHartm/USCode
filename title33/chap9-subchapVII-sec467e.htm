§467e. Existing liabilities and obligations unaffected

Nothing contained in this subchapter and no action or failure to act under this subchapter shall be construed (1) to create any liability in the United States or its officers or employees for the recovery of damages caused by such action of failure to act; or (2) to relieve an owner or operator of a dam of the legal duties, obligations, or liabilities incident to the ownership or operation of the dam.

(Pub. L. 92–367, §6, Aug. 8, 1972, 86 Stat. 507.)
