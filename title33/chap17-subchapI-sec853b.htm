§853b. Promotion by selection to grades above lieutenant (junior grade)

Promotion to fill vacancies in all permanent grades above that of lieutenant (junior grade) shall be made by selection from the next lower respective grades upon recommendation of the personnel board hereinafter provided for.

(June 3, 1948, ch. 390, §3, 62 Stat. 298.)
