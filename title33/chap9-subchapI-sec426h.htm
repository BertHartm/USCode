§426h. “Shores” defined

As used in sections 426e to 426h of this title, the word “shores” includes all the shorelines of the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, the Gulf of Mexico, the Great Lakes, and lakes, estuaries, and bays directly connected therewith.

(Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 960, §4, 60 Stat. 1057; July 28, 1956, ch. 768, 70 Stat. 703.)
