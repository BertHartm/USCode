§853f. Length of service for promotion purposes

Each officer shall be assumed to have, for promotion purposes, at least the same length of service as any officer below him on the lineal list, except that an officer who has lost numbers shall be assumed to have for promotion purposes no greater service than the officer next above him in his new position on the lineal list.

(June 3, 1948, ch. 390, §7, 62 Stat. 298.)
