§3305. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this chapter $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2013.

(Pub. L. 110–114, title IX, §9006, Nov. 8, 2007, 121 Stat. 1291.)

“Secretary” Defined

Secretary means the Secretary of the Army, see section 2 of Pub. L. 110–114, set out as a note under section 2201 of this title.
