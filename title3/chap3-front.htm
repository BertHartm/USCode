Amendments

1977—Pub. L. 95–179, Nov. 15, 1977, 91 Stat. 1371, substituted “United States Secret Service Uniformed Division” for “Executive Protective Service” in chapter heading and in item 202.

1975—Pub. L. 94–196, §1(d)(2), Dec. 31, 1975, 89 Stat. 1110, added item 208 and renumbered former item 208 as 209.

1970—Pub. L. 91–217, §1(2), Mar. 19, 1970, 84 Stat. 74, substituted “Executive Protective Service” for “White House Police” in chapter heading and in item 202.

1951—Act July 16, 1951, ch. 226, §5(b), 65 Stat. 122, struck out item 201 “Protection of President and family authorized”.
