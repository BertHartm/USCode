Amendment of Analysis

Pub. L. 109–59, title IV, §4305(a), Aug. 10, 2005, 119 Stat. 1764, provided that, effective on the first January 1st occurring more than 12 months after Aug. 10, 2005, the item relating to section 14504 in the analysis for this chapter is repealed.
