§32510. Annual report

Not later than March 31 of each year, the Secretary of Transportation shall submit to Congress and the President a report on the progress in carrying out section 32501 of this title. The report shall include—

(1) a statement of the cost savings resulting from carrying out this chapter; and

(2) recommendations for legislative or other action the Secretary decides may be appropriate.

(Pub. L. 103–272, §1(e), July 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 1047.)
