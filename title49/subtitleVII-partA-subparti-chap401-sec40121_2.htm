§40121.1 Interstate agreements for airport facilities

Congress consents to a State making an agreement, not in conflict with a law of the United States, with another State to develop or operate an airport facility.

(Added Pub. L. 104–287, §5(69)(A), Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3395.)

Historical and Revision Notes

This restates 49:44502(e) as 49:40121 to provide a more appropriate place in title 49.
