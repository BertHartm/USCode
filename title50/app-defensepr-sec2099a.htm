§2099a. Civil-military integration

An important purpose of this title [sections 2091 to 2099a of this title] is the creation of production capacity that will remain economically viable after guarantees and other assistance provided under this title [said sections] have expired.

(Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, title III, §310, as added Pub. L. 102–558, title I, §125, Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 4208.)

Effective Date

Section deemed to have become effective Mar. 1, 1992, see section 304 of Pub. L. 102–558, set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment note under section 2062 of this Appendix.

Termination Date

Termination of section, see section 2166(a) of this Appendix.
