§585. Omitted

Codification

Section, act Oct. 17, 1940, ch. 888, art. VI, §605, 54 Stat. 1191, related to the inapplicability of the Soldiers’ and Sailors’ Relief Act of 1918 [section 101 et seq. of this Appendix], to military service performed after Oct. 17, 1940.
