§441d. Application

This subchapter shall cease to be effective on January 6, 1998.

(July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title IX, §905, as added Jan. 6, 1996, Pub. L. 104–93, title III, §303(a), 109 Stat. 965; amended Oct. 11, 1996, Pub. L. 104–293, title III, §304, 110 Stat. 3464.)

Amendments

1996—Pub. L. 104–293 substituted “on January 6, 1998” for “on the date which is one year after January 6, 1996”.
