§441d. Application

This subchapter shall cease to be effective on the date which is one year after January 6, 1996.

(July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title IX, §905, as added Jan. 6, 1996, Pub. L. 104–93, title III, §303(a), 109 Stat. 965.)
