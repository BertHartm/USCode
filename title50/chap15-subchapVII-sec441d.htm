§441d. Application

This subchapter shall cease to be effective on January 6, 2000.

(July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title IX, §905, as added Pub. L. 104–93, title III, §303(a), Jan. 6, 1996, 109 Stat. 965; amended Pub. L. 104–293, title III, §304, Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3464; Pub. L. 105–107, title III, §304, Nov. 20, 1997, 111 Stat. 2252; Pub. L. 105–272, title III, §303, Oct. 20, 1998, 112 Stat. 2400.)

Amendments

1998—Pub. L. 105–272 substituted “January 6, 2000” for “January 6, 1999”.

1997—Pub. L. 105–107 substituted “January 6, 1999” for “January 6, 1998”.

1996—Pub. L. 104–293 substituted “on January 6, 1998” for “on the date which is one year after January 6, 1996”.
