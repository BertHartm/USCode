EXPORTATION RESTRICTIONS ON CERTAIN ARTICLES

ACT JULY 2, 1940, CH. 508, 54 STAT. 714

§701. Repealed. Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, §53, 70A Stat. 641

Section, acts July 2, 1940, ch. 508, §6, 54 Stat. 714; June 30, 1942, ch. 461, 56 Stat. 463; July 1, 1944, ch. 360, 58 Stat. 671; June 30, 1945, ch. 205, 59 Stat. 270; May 23, 1946, ch. 269, 60 Stat. 215; June 30, 1947, ch. 184, §1, 61 Stat. 214; July 15, 1947, ch. 248, §4, 61 Stat. 323; Dec. 30, 1947, ch. 526, §3(a), 61 Stat. 946, authorized President, until Feb. 28, 1949, to prohibit or curtail exportation of any articles, technical data, materials, or supplies, except under those rules and regulations as he should prescribe. See section 2401 et seq. of this Appendix.

§702. Omitted

Codification

Section, act May 28, 1941, ch. 134, 55 Stat. 206, extended application of section 701 of this Appendix to territories and possessions of the United States.
