§1742. Repealed. Pub. L. 94–412, title V, §501(g), Sept. 14, 1976, 90 Stat. 1258

Section, acts Mar. 8, 1946, ch. 82, §9, 60 Stat. 46; Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 1013, 70 Stat. 1068, made provision for price adjustments on prior sales of surplus war-built vessels to citizens.

Savings Provision

Repeal not to affect any action taken or proceeding pending at the time of repeal, see section 501(h) of Pub. L. 94–412, set out as a note under section 1601 of Title 50, War and National Defense.
