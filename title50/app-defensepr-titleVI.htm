TITLE VI—CONTROL OF REAL ESTATE CREDIT

Codification

Sections 2132 to 2137 of this Appendix, which comprised this title, terminated on June 30, 1953, by the terms of section 2166(a) of this Appendix. Previous to such termination, act June 30, 1952, ch. 530, title I, §116(a), 66 Stat. 305 amended catchline of title to omit reference to consumer credit control and subtitle heading of this title to read “This title authorizes the regulation of real estate construction credit only.”

§2131. Repealed. June 30, 1952, ch. 530, title I, §116(a), 66 Stat. 305

Section, acts Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, title VI, §601, 64 Stat. 812; July 31, 1951, ch. 275, title I, §106(a), 65 Stat. 138, related to power to exercise consumer credit controls.

§§2132 to 2137. Omitted

Section 2134, act Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, title VI, §604, 64 Stat. 814, related to consumer credit controls.
