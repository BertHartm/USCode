§1806. Omitted

Codification

Section, act Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 243, title VI, §601, 60 Stat. 140, required an executive agreement to be entered into between the Presidents of the United States and the Philippines as a condition precedent for certain private-aid payments under sections 1751 to 1763 of this Appendix which have been omitted from the Code.
