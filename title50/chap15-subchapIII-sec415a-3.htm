§415a–3. Repealed. Pub. L. 111–259, title III, §325(c)(2), Oct. 7, 2010, 124 Stat. 2683

Section, Pub. L. 111–118, div. A, title VIII, §8104, Dec. 19, 2009, 123 Stat. 3451, required the Director of National Intelligence annually to submit a future-years intelligence program (including associated annexes) reflecting estimated expenditures and proposed appropriations. See section 415a–9 of this title.

Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation act:

Pub. L. 110–329, div. C, title VIII, §8112, Sept. 30, 2008, 122 Stat. 3645.
