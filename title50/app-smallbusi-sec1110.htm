§1110. Transferred

Codification

Section, act June 11, 1942, ch. 404, §10, 56 Stat. 356, which related to insured banks as depositaries of public money, duties and security of these banks, prohibition of discrimination between banks, and to repeal of inconsistent laws, was transferred to section 265 of Title 12, Banks and Banking.
