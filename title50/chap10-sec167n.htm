§167n. Reports

The Secretary of the Interior is directed to report annually to the Congress on the matters contained in this chapter.

(Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 426, §16, as added Pub. L. 86–777, §2, Sept. 13, 1960, 74 Stat. 923.)
