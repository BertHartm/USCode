§1989c. Short title

This title [sections 1989c to 1989c–8 of this Appendix] may be cited as the “Aleutian and Pribilof Islands Restitution Act”.

(Pub. L. 100–383, title II, §201, Aug. 10, 1988, 102 Stat. 911.)
