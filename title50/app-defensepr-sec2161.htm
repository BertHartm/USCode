§2161. Authorization of appropriations; availability of funds

(a) Authorization

Except as provided in subsection (b), there are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary and appropriate for the carrying out of the provisions and purposes of this Act [sections 2061 to 2171 of this Appendix] (including sections 302 and 303 [sections 2092 and 2093 of this Appendix], but excluding sections 305 and 306 [sections 2095 and 2096 of this Appendix]) by the President and such agencies as he may designate or create. Funds made available pursuant to this paragraph for the purposes of this Act [said sections] may be allocated or transferred for any of the purposes of this Act [said sections], with the approval of the Office of Management and Budget, to any agency designated to assist in carrying out this Act [said sections]. Funds so allocated or transferred shall remain available for such period as may be specified in the Acts making such funds available.

(b) Title III authorization

There are authorized to be appropriated for each of fiscal years 1996 through 2008, such sums as may be necessary to carry out title III [sections 2091 to 2099a of this Appendix].
