§2163. Territorial application of Act

The provisions of this Act [sections 2061 to 2171 of this Appendix] shall be applicable to the United States, its Territories and possessions, and the District of Columbia.

(Sept. 8, 1950, ch. 932, title VII, §713, 64 Stat. 821.)
