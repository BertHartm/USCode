§1320c–11. Exemptions of Christian Science sanatoriums

The provisions of this part shall not apply with respect to a Christian Science sanatorium operated, or listed and certified, by the First Church of Christ, Scientist, Boston, Massachusetts.

(Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XI, §1162, as added Sept. 3, 1982, Pub. L. 97–248, title I, §143, 96 Stat. 393.)
