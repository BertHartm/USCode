§300ff–29. Technical assistance

The Secretary may provide technical assistance in administering and coordinating the activities authorized under section 300ff–22 of this title.

(July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXVI, §2619, as added Aug. 18, 1990, Pub. L. 101–381, title II, §201, 104 Stat. 597.)
