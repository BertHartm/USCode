§9905. Office of Community Services; establishment; function

(a) There is established in the Department of Health and Human Services an Office of Community Services. The Office shall be headed by a Director.

(b) The Secretary shall carry out his functions under this chapter through the Office of Community Services established in subsection (a) of this section.

(Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, §676, Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 516.)
