§5667e–7. Grant cycles

Grants under this part shall be made for 3-year periods.

(Pub. L. 93–415, title II, §288G, as added Pub. L. 102–586, §2(i)(1)(C), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5012.)
