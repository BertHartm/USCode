§11450. Termination

The provisions of this part other than section 1 738 [42 U.S.C. 11448] and 740 2 shall terminate on December 31, 1999.

(Pub. L. 100–77, title VII, §741, July 22, 1987, 101 Stat. 532; Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, §621(c), Nov. 29, 1990, 104 Stat. 4744; Pub. L. 102–590, §11(b)(2), Nov. 10, 1992, 106 Stat. 5141; Pub. L. 103–239, title VII, §701(b), May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 606; Pub. L. 104–110, title I, §102(d)(2), Feb. 13, 1996, 110 Stat. 769; Pub. L. 105–114, title II, §203(c)(2), Nov. 21, 1997, 111 Stat. 2288.)

References in Text

Section 740, referred to in text, is section 740 of Pub. L. 100–77, title VII, July 22, 1987, 101 Stat. 531, which amended sections 1503 and 1551 of Title 29, Labor.

Amendments

1997—Pub. L. 105–114 substituted “1999” for “1997”.

1996—Pub. L. 104–110 substituted “December 31, 1997” for “October 1, 1995”.

1994—Pub. L. 103–239 substituted “1995” for “1993”.

1992—Pub. L. 102–590 inserted “738 and” before “740”.

1990—Pub. L. 101–645 substituted “1993” for “1990”.
