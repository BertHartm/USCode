§5118e. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general

For the purpose of carrying out this subchapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $12,500,000 for fiscal year 1992, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1995.

(b) Availability of appropriations

Amounts appropriated under subsection (a) of this section shall remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 93–247, title III, §306, as added Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, §661(b), Nov. 29, 1990, 104 Stat. 4760; amended Pub. L. 102–295, title I, §131, May 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 199.)

Amendments

1992—Subsec. (a). Pub. L. 102–295 inserted before period at end “, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1995”.
