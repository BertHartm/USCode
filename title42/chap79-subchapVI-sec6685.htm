§6685. Expiration

The provisions of this subchapter shall cease to be effective on December 31, 2000, and the panel shall terminate on that date.

(Pub. L. 94–282, title VI, §605, as added Pub. L. 101–189, div. A, title VIII, §841(a)(1), Nov. 29, 1989, 103 Stat. 1512.)
