§11449. Authorization of appropriations

(a) Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1994 and 1995.

(b) Ratable reduction

If for any fiscal year the appropriation is less than the amount authorized in paragraph (1) to carry out the provisions of this part, the amount available in such fiscal year for the programs under this part shall be ratably reduced.

(c) Special rule

Nothing in this part shall be construed to require the Secretary to carry out the provisions of this part from funds appropriated for programs other than funds appropriated for this part.

“(3) $14,800,000 for fiscal year 1993.”

Subsec. (b). Pub. L. 102–590, §11(b)(1)(B), which directed amendment of subsec.
