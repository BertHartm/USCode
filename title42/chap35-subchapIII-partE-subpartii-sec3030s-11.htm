§§3030s–11, 3030s–12. Omitted

Codification

Section 3030s–11, Pub. L. 89–73, title III, §375, as added Pub. L. 106–501, title III, §316(2), Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2256, which provided for an innovation grant program, was effective for 3 fiscal years after Nov. 13, 2000.

Section 3030s–12, Pub. L. 89–73, title III, §376, as added Pub. L. 106–501, title III, §316(2), Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2257, which directed Assistant Secretary to carry out activities of national significance to promote quality and continuous improvement in the support provided to family and other informal caregivers of older individuals, was effective for 3 fiscal years after November 13, 2000.
