§678. Repealed. Pub. L. 101–508, title V, §5052(b), Nov. 5, 1990, 104 Stat. 1388–228

Section, act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title IV, §478, as added Oct. 22, 1986, Pub. L. 99–514, title XVIII, §1883(b)(10)(A), 100 Stat. 2917, excluded from AFDC unit child for whom foster care maintenance payments are made.

Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable with respect to benefits for months beginning on or after the first day of the sixth calendar month following November 1990, see section 5052(c) of Pub. L. 101–508, set out as an Effective Date note under section 609 of this title.
