§11909. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $175,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and $182,350,000 for fiscal year 1994. Any amount appropriated under this section shall remain available until expended.

(b) Set-asides

Of any amount made available in any fiscal year to carry out this subchapter, not more than 6.25 percent of such amount shall be available for grants for federally assisted, low-income housing. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, of any amounts appropriated for drug elimination grants under this subchapter for fiscal years 1993 and 1994, not more than 6.25 percent shall be available for grants for federally assisted low-income housing and 5.0 percent shall be available for public housing youth sports program grants under section 11903a of this title.

(c) Set-aside for youth sports programs

Of any amount made available in any fiscal year to carry out this subchapter, 5 percent of such amount shall be available for public housing youth sports program grants under section 11903a of this title for such fiscal year.

(Pub. L. 100–690, title V, §5130, as added Pub. L. 101–625, title V, §581(a), Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 4248; amended Pub. L. 102–550, title I, §§126(a), 161(a), (b), Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 3710, 3718.)
