§5116i. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter, $66,000,000 for fiscal year 1997 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1998 through 2001.

(Pub. L. 93–247, title II, §210, as added Pub. L. 104–235, title I, §121, Oct. 3, 1996, 110 Stat. 3088.)
