§3056i. Treatment of assistance

Assistance furnished under this subchapter shall not be construed to be financial assistance described in section 1255a(h)(1)(A) of title 8.

(Pub. L. 89–73, title V, §511, as added Pub. L. 106–501, title V, §501, Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2280.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 3056i, Pub. L. 89–73, title V, §511, as added Pub. L. 102–375, title V, §507, Sept. 30, 1992, 106 Stat. 1269, related to treatment of assistance, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 106–501.
