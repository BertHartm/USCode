§1437aaa–6. Relationship to other homeownership opportunities

The program authorized under this subchapter shall be in addition to any other public housing homeownership and management opportunities, including opportunities under section 1437c(h) of this title and subchapter II of this chapter.

(Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 896, title III, §307, as added Nov. 28, 1990, Pub. L. 101–625, title IV, §411, 104 Stat. 4159.)
