§1490g. Repealed. Pub. L. 98–181, title V, §503(c), Nov. 30, 1983, 97 Stat. 1241

Section, act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title V, §527, as added Aug. 22, 1974, Pub. L. 93–383, title V, §518, 88 Stat. 699, defined “housing” as including mobile homes and mobile home sites, and authorized the Secretary to prescribe property standards for mobile homes financed under this subchapter.
