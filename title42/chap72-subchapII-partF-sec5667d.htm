§5667d. “Juvenile” defined

For the purposes of this part, the term “juvenile” means a person who is less than 18 years of age.

(Pub. L. 93–415, title II, §287, as added Pub. L. 102–586, §2(i)(1)(C), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5008.)
