§3796hh–5. Training and technical assistance

Of the total amounts appropriated under this subchapter, not less than 5 percent and up to 8 percent shall be available for providing training and technical assistance relating to the purpose areas of this subchapter to improve the capacity of grantees and other entities.

(Pub. L. 90–351, title I, §2106, as added Pub. L. 109–162, title I, §102(d), Jan. 5, 2006, 119 Stat. 2978.)
