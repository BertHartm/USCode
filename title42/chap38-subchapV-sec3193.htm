§3193. Repealed. Pub. L. 94–519, §6, Oct. 17, 1976, 90 Stat. 2456

Section, Pub. L. 89–136, title V, §514, as added Pub. L. 93–423, §11, Sept. 27, 1974, 88 Stat. 1162, provided for the acquisition and disposal of excess property from the Administrator of General Services by the Federal cochairman of each regional action planning commission.

Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 17, 1977, see section 9 of Pub. L. 94–519, set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under section 484 of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.
