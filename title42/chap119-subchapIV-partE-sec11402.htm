§11402. Administrative provisions

The provisions of, and regulations and procedures applicable under, section 5304(g) of this title shall apply to assistance and projects under this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 100–77, title IV, §443, as added Pub. L. 100–628, title IV, §482(a), Nov. 7, 1988, 102 Stat. 3238.)
