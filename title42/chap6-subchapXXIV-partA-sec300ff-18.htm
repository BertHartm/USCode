§300ff–18. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to make grants under this part, $275,000,000 in each of the fiscal years 1991 and 1992, and such sums as may be necessary in each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1995.

(July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXVI, §2608, as added Aug. 18, 1990, Pub. L. 101–381, title I, §101(3), 104 Stat. 585.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 2608 of act July 1, 1944, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see section 238g of this title.
