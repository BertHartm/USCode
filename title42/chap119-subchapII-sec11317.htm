§11317. Definitions

For purposes of this subchapter:

(1) The term “Council” means the Interagency Council on the Homeless established in section 11311 of this title.

(2) The term “Federal agency” has the meaning given the term “agency” in section 551(1) of title 5.

(Pub. L. 100–77, title II, §207, July 22, 1987, 101 Stat. 489.)
