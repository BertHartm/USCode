§13261. Prohibited acts

It shall be unlawful for any person to violate any provision of section 13251, 13253(b), or 13257 of this title, or any regulation issued under such sections.

(Pub. L. 102–486, title V, §511, Oct. 24, 1992, 106 Stat. 2899.)
