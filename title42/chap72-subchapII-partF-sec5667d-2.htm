§5667d–2. Administrative requirements

The Administrator shall administer this part subject to the requirements of sections 5665a, 5673, and 5676 of this title.

(Pub. L. 93–415, title II, §287B, as added Pub. L. 102–586, §2(i)(1)(C), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5009.)
