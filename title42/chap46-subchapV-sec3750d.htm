§3750d. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated $10,000,000 to carry out sections 3750a to 3750d of this title for each of the fiscal years 1993, 1994, and 1995.

(Pub. L. 102–519, title I, §133, Oct. 25, 1992, 106 Stat. 3387.)

Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Anti Car Theft Act of 1992, and not as part of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 which comprises this chapter.

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 3750a, 3750b, 3750c of this title.
