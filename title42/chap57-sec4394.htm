§4394. Supplemental reports by President

The President shall, within one year of his transmittal to the Congress of the report required by section 4393 of this title, and annually thereafter, supplement that report with such new data, evaluations, or recommendations as he may deem appropriate.

(Pub. L. 91–515, title V, §501(d), Oct. 30, 1970, 84 Stat. 1310.)
