§3255. Repealed. Pub. L. 91–512, title I, §104(a), Oct. 26, 1970, 84 Stat. 1228

Section, Pub. L. 89–272, title II, §206, Oct. 20, 1965, 79 Stat. 999, authorized grants to State and interstate agencies for surveys of solid-waste disposal practices and problems, and for development of solid-waste disposal plans.
