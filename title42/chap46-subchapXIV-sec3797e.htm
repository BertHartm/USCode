§3797e. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2001 through 2003.

(Pub. L. 90–351, title I, §2705, as added Pub. L. 106–386, div. B, title I, §1108(b), Oct. 28, 2000, 114 Stat. 1502.)
