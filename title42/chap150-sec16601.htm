§16601. Definitions

In this chapter:

(1) Administrator

The term “Administrator” means the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.

(2) ISS

The term “ISS” means the International Space Station.

(3) NASA

The term “NASA” means the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.

(Pub. L. 109–155, §2, Dec. 30, 2005, 119 Stat. 2897.)

References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning Pub. L. 109–155, Dec. 30, 2005, 119 Stat. 2895, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.

Short Title

Pub.
