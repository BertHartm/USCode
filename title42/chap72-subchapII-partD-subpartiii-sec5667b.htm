§5667b. “Juvenile” defined

For purposes of this part, the term “juvenile” means an individual who is less than 22 years of age.

(Pub. L. 93–415, title II, §283, as added Pub. L. 102–586, §2(i), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5006.)
