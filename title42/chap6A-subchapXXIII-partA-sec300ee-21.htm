§300ee–21. Evaluations

The Secretary shall, directly or through grants or contracts, evaluate the services provided and activities carried out with payments to States under this part.

(July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXV, formerly title XV, §2511, as added Nov. 4, 1988, Pub. L. 100–607, title II, §221, 102 Stat. 3101; renumbered title XXV, Aug. 16, 1989, Pub. L. 101–93, §5(e)(1), 103 Stat. 612.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 2511 of act July 1, 1944, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts, see section 238j of this title.
