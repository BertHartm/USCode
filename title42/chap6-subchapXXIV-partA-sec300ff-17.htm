§300ff–17. Definitions

For purposes of this part:

(1) Eligible area

The term “eligible area” means a metropolitan area described in section 300ff–11(a) of this title.

(2) Metropolitan area

The term “metropolitan area” means an area referred to in the HIV/AIDS Surveillance Report of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention as a metropolitan area.

(July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXVI, §2607, as added Aug. 18, 1990, Pub. L. 101–381, title I, §101(3), 104 Stat. 585; amended Nov. 15, 1990, Pub. L. 101–557, title IV, §401(b)(1), 104 Stat. 2771; Oct. 27, 1992, Pub. L. 102–531, title III, §312(d)(28), 106 Stat. 3506.)

A prior section 2607 of act July 1, 1944, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see section 238f of this title.
