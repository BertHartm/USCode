§2297b–12. Security

Any references to the term “Commission” or to the Department in sections 2201(k), 2271(a), and 2278b of this title shall be considered to include the Corporation.

(Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, §1313, as added Oct. 24, 1992, Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, §901, 106 Stat. 2933.)

Repeal of Section

Section repealed effective as of date on which 100 percent of ownership of United States Enrichment Corporation has been transferred to private investors, see section 3116(a)(1) of Pub. L. 104–134, set out as a note under section 2297 of this title.
