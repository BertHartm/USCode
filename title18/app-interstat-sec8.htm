§8. Effective Date

This Act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after the date of its enactment.

(Pub. L. 91–538, §8, Dec. 9, 1970, 84 Stat. 1403.)

References in Text

The date of its enactment, referred to in text, means Dec. 9, 1970.
