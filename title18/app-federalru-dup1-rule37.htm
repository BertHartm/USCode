[Rule 37. Taking Appeal; and Petition for Writ of Certiorari] (Abrogated Dec. 4, 1967, eff. July 1, 1968)

Notes of Advisory Committee on Rules—1967

These are the criminal rules [Rules 37, 38(b), (c), 39] relating to appeals, the provisions of which are transferred to and covered by the Federal Rules of Appellate Procedure and (in the case of Rule 37(b) and (c)) by the Rules of the Supreme Court.
