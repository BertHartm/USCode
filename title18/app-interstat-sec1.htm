§1. Short title

This Act may be cited as the “Interstate Agreement on Detainers Act”.

(Pub. L. 91–538, §1, Dec. 9, 1970, 84 Stat. 1397.)

Codification

The Interstate Agreement on Detainers is also set out in sections 24–701 to 24–705 of the District of Columbia Code.
