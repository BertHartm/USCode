Savings Provision

Subsec. (l) of section 56 of act Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, 65 Stat. 730, provided that the repeal of this section should not affect any rights or liabilities existing hereunder on the effective date of the repeal (Oct. 31, 1951).
