Rule 58.1. Notice of Appeal

Review of a decision of this court shall be obtained by filing with the clerk an original and the requisite number of copies of a notice of appeal (but not fewer than four) within the time and manner prescribed for appeals to United States courts of appeal as provided for in Rule 3 of the Federal Rules of Appellate Procedure, together with the fee provided in RCFC 77.1(c)(2) of the Rules of the United States Court of Federal Claims.

Rules Committee Note

Although the rule has no FRCP counterpart, it is a necessary component of the court's rules because it prescribes the time and manner for the filing of an appeal from a decision of this court.
