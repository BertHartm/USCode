Rule 73. Time for Filing Documents—Notice of Filing

(a) Time. Upon motion of a party for good cause shown, or upon its own initiative, the court may shorten or extend the times for filing prescribed in the rules of this title.

(b) Notice. The clerk shall give notice to all parties of the date on which the record is filed.
