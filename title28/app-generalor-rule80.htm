Rule 80. Record or Transcript as Evidence

Whenever the testimony of a witness at a trial or hearing which was reported is admissible in evidence at a later trial, it may be proved by the transcript thereof duly certified by the person who reported the testimony.

Rules Committee Note

Former subdivisions (a), (b) and (c) were deleted and minor changes were made to former subdivision (d) in order to more closely conform the rule to FRCP 80.
