Rule 15.1. Briefs and Oral Argument in National Labor Relations Board Proceedings

Each party adverse to the National Labor Relations Board in an enforcement or a review proceeding shall proceed first on briefing and at oral argument unless the court orders otherwise.

(As added Mar. 10, 1986, eff. July 1, 1986.)

Notes of Advisory Committee on Rules—1986

This rule simply confirms the existing practice in most circuits.
