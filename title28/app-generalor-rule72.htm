Rules Committee Note

Chapter IX of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, titled “Special Proceedings,” (comprising FRCP 71A—FRCP 73) has not been included in the main body of the court's rules. Instead, rules relating to the court's special proceedings appear in the appendices to the rules.
