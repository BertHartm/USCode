Rule 26.1. Costs

All costs, charges, and expenses incident to taking depositions shall be borne by the party making application for the same unless otherwise provided for by stipulation or by order of the court.

(Added Sept. 30, 2003, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.)

Practice Comment

Pursuant to the renumbering of the Rules, the former Rule 26(h) now will be identified as Rule 26.1.
