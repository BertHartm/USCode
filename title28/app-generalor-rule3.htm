Rule 3. Commencement of Action

A civil action is commenced by filing a complaint with the court. See RCFC 40.2(a).

Rules Committee Note

In the interest of achieving greater uniformity with the corresponding federal rule, a number of changes have been made to RCFC 3. First, subdivision (a) was fully conformed to the federal rule; the reference to RCFC 40.2 calls attention to this court's “related case” rule. Second, former subdivision (b), which addressed disputes regarding filing dates, was deleted—both in the interest of uniformity and in the belief that it was inappropriate to include a rule of decision as part of a procedural rule. Third, former subdivision (c) (prescribing a cover sheet and identifying the number of copies required for filing), was moved to RCFC 5.3(d).
