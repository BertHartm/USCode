Rule 57. Declaratory Judgments

The procedure for obtaining a declaratory judgment pursuant to Title 28 U.S.C. §2201, shall be in accordance with these rules and the right to trial by jury may be demanded under the circumstances and in the manner prescribed by Rules 38 and 39. The existence of another adequate remedy does not preclude a judgment for declaratory relief in cases where it is appropriate. The court may order a speedy hearing of an action for declaratory judgment.

(As amended Dec. 18, 2001, eff. Apr. 1, 2002.)
