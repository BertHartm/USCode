§1245. Omitted

Codification

Section, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, §15, 47 Stat. 769; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, §15, 48 Stat. 464, related to statutes continued in force, and was omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.
