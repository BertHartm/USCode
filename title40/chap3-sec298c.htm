§298c. Repealed. Pub. L. 86–249, §17(18), Sept. 9, 1959, 73 Stat. 486

Section, act June 16, 1949, ch. 218, title IV, §406, 63 Stat. 199, authorized expenditure of not more than $1,800 for landscaping of grounds of any public building.

Savings Provision

Section repealed except as to its application to any project referred to in section 613 of this title, see section 17 of Pub. L. 86–249, set out as a note under section 341 of this title.
