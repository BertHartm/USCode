§490h. Flexiplace work telecommuting centers; revenues to defray costs

The Administrator of General Services is authorized to accept and retain income received by the General Services Administration on or after October 1, 1993, from Federal agencies and non-Federal sources, to defray costs directly associated with the functions of flexiplace work telecommuting centers.

(Pub. L. 104–52, title IV, §5, Nov. 19, 1995, 109 Stat. 486.)

Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Treasury, Postal Service, and General Government Appropriations Act, 1996, and not as part of the Federal Property and Administrative Services Act of 1949, part of which comprises this chapter.
