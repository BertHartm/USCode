§662. Repealed. Pub. L. 89–774, §5(b), Nov. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 1353

Section, Pub. L. 86–669, title II, §202, July 14, 1960, 74 Stat. 538; Pub. L. 89–173, §7, Sept. 8, 1965, 79 Stat. 666, established an Advisory Board of National Capital Transportation Agency, composed of seven members, provided for their appointment and compensation, and prescribed their duties. The Board and the Agency were superseded by Board of Directors of Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority and such Authority, respectively.
