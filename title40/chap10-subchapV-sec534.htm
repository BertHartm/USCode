§534. Waiver of procedures for disposal of urban lands, acquisition or change of use of real property

The procedures prescribed in sections 532 and 533 of this title may be waived during any period of national emergency proclaimed by the President.

(June 30, 1949, ch. 288, title VIII, §805, as added Oct. 16, 1968, Pub. L. 90–577, title V, §501, 82 Stat. 1105.)
