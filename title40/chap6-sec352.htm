§§352 to 354. Repealed. Pub. L. 86–249, §17(18), Sept. 9, 1959, 73 Stat. 486

Section 352, act June 16, 1949, ch. 218, title I, §101, 63 Stat. 176, related to comprehensive planning of Federal public buildings outside the District of Columbia, cooperation between the Administrator of General Services and the Postmaster General, and equitable distribution of projects. See section 601 et seq. of this title.

Section 353, act June 16, 1949, ch. 218, title I, §102, 63 Stat. 176, stated Congressional declaration of purpose for equitable distribution of selected projects.

Section 354, act June 16, 1949, ch. 218, title I, §103, 63 Stat. 176, authorized appropriations for carrying out the purposes of sections 352 and 353 of this title.

Savings Provision

Sections repealed except as to their application to any project referred to in section 613 of this title, see section 17 of Pub. L. 86–249, set out as a note under section 341 of this title.
