§§57, 58. Omitted

Codification

Section 57, R.S. §1804; act Feb. 11, 1895, ch. 79, 28 Stat. 650, which related to punishment for breaking or destroying pipes, hydrants, etc., in the city of Washington, was omitted as not having general applicability.

Section 58, R.S. §1806; act Feb. 11, 1895, ch. 79, 28 Stat. 650, which related to punishment for maliciously making water impure in the city of Washington, was omitted as not having general applicability.
