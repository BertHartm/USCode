§318c. Penalties

Whoever shall violate any rule or regulation promulgated pursuant to section 318a of this title shall be fined not more 1 $50 or imprisoned not more than thirty days, or both.

(June 1, 1948, ch. 359, §4, 62 Stat. 281.)
