§884. Separability

If any provisions of this chapter or the application thereof to any body, agency, situation, or circumstances is held invalid the remainder of the chapter and the application of such provision to other bodies, agencies, situations, or circumstances shall not be affected thereby.

(Pub. L. 92–578, §16, Oct. 27, 1972, 86 Stat. 1274.)
