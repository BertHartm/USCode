§304k. Retention or delivery of abandoned or forfeited property deemed sale with respect to informer's fees and mitigation of forfeiture

Retention or delivery of forfeited or abandoned property under sections 304f to 304m of this title shall be regarded as the sale thereof for the purpose of laws providing for informer's fees or remission or mitigation of any forfeiture. Any property so acquired when no longer needed for official use shall be disposed of in the same manner as other surplus property.

(Aug. 27, 1935, ch. 740, title III, §306, 49 Stat. 880.)
