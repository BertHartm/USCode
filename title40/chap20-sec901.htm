§901. Monitoring system

The head of each executive agency, including the Department of Defense, shall designate one office, officer, or employee of the agency to establish and operate a central monitoring system for, and provide oversight of, the motor vehicle operations of the agency, related activities, and related reporting requirements.

(Pub. L. 99–272, title XV, §15301, Apr. 7, 1986, 100 Stat. 335.)
