§1472. Evaluation criteria and plans

(a) Measurable test criteria

The head of each executive agency conducting a pilot program under section 1471 of this title shall establish, to the maximum extent practicable, measurable criteria for evaluating the effects of the procedures or techniques to be tested under the program.

(b) Test plan

Before a pilot program may be conducted under section 1471 of this title, the Administrator shall submit to Congress a detailed test plan for the program, including a detailed description of the procedures to be used and a list of any regulations that are to be waived.

(Pub. L. 104–106, div. E, title LIII, §5302, Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 691.)
