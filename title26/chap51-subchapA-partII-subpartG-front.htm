Repeal of Subpart

Pub. L. 109–59, title XI, §11125(a)(1)(E), (c), Aug. 10, 2005, 119 Stat. 1953, 1957, provided that, effective July 1, 2008, but inapplicable to taxes imposed for periods before such date, this subpart, other than sections 5142, 5143, 5145, and 5146 of this title, is repealed.

Prior Provisions

A prior subpart G, consisting of sections 5141 to 5149, related to general provisions, prior to the general revision of this chapter by Pub. L. 85–859, title II, §201, Sept. 2, 1958, 72 Stat. 1313.

Amendments

2004—Pub. L. 108–357, title II, §246(c), Oct. 22, 2004, 118 Stat. 1449, added items 5148 and 5149 and struck out former item 5148 “Cross references”.

1976—Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, §1905(b)(3)(D)(ii), Oct. 4, 1976, 90 Stat. 1822, struck out item 5144 “Supply of stamps”.
