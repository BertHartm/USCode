§485k. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Reclamation Project Act of 1939.”

(Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418, §19, 53 Stat. 1198.)

References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning act Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418, 53 Stat. 1187, as amended, which enacted this subchapter, sections 375a, 380a, and 387 to 389 of this title and section 16d of Title 41, Public Contracts, and enacted provision set out as a note under section 485j of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
