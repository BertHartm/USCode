§31b. Definitions

As used in sections 31a to 31h of this title:

(1) The term “advisory committee” means the advisory committee established under section 31d of this title.

(2) The term “Director” means the Director of the United States Geological Survey.

(3) The term “geologic mapping program” means the National Cooperative Geologic Mapping Program established by section 31c(a) of this title.

(4) The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(5) The term “Survey” means the United States Geological Survey.

(Pub. L. 102–285, §3, May 18, 1992, 106 Stat. 167.)
