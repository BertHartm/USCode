§31h. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 31a to 31h of this title the following:

(1) For Federal mapping activities under sections 31a to 31h of this title, $12,500,000 for fiscal year 1993, $14,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, $16,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and $18,000,000 for fiscal year 1996.

(2) For Federal support activities under sections 31a to 31h of this title, $9,500,000 for fiscal year 1993, $10,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, $10,500,000 for fiscal year 1995, and $11,000,000 for fiscal year 1996.

(3) For State mapping activities under sections 31a to 31h of this title, $15,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, $18,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, $21,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and $25,000,000 for fiscal year 1996.

(4) For educational support activities under sections 31a to 31h of this title, $500,000 for fiscal year 1993, $750,000 for fiscal year 1994, $1,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and $1,500,000 for fiscal year 1996.

(Pub. L. 102–285, §9, May 18, 1992, 106 Stat. 171.)

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 31a, 31b, 31d, 31f, 31g of this title.
