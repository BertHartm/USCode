§122f . Rules and regulations

The committee shall prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of sections 122b to 122g of this title.

(Pub. L. 95–391, title I, §111, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 777.)

Codification

Section is based on section 5 of House Resolution No. 687, Ninety-fifth Congress, Sept. 20, 1977, which was enacted into permanent law by Pub. L. 95–391.

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 122b, 122c, 122d, 122e, 122g of this title.
