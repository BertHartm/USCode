§286g. Availability of contingent fund of House

Until such time as funds are appropriated by law to carry out the purpose of this chapter, the contingent fund of the House shall be available for such purpose.

(Pub. L. 93–554, title I, ch. III, §101, Dec. 27, 1974, 88 Stat. 1777.)

Codification

Section is based on section 203(h) of House Resolution No. 988, Ninety-third Congress, Oct. 8, 1974, which was enacted into permanent law by Pub. L. 93–554.
