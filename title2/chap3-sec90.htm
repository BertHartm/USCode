§90. Removal from office of employees of House

The violation of any of the provisions of sections 85 to 87 and 89 of this title shall, upon ascertainment thereof, be deemed to be cause for removal from office.

(Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 830, §1, 31 Stat. 968.)
