§263 . Receipts for contributions

Every individual who receives a contribution of $500 or more for any of the purposes hereinafter designated shall within five days after receipt thereof rendered 1 to the person or organization for which such contribution was received a detailed account thereof, including the name and address of the person making such contribution and the date on which received.

(Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, §304, 60 Stat. 840.)

1 So in original. Probably should be “render”.
