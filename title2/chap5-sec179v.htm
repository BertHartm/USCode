§179v. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Librarian such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of sections 179l to 179w of this title, but in no fiscal year shall such sum exceed $250,000.

(Pub. L. 104–285, title I, §112, Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3382.)

Termination of Section

For termination of section, see section 179w of this title.

Section Referred to in Other Sections

This section is referred to in sections 179l, 179s, 179t, 179u, 179w of this title.
