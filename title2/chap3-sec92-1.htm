§92–1. Clerk hire allowance payments; place of performance of services

No person shall be paid from any clerk hire allowance if such person does not perform the services for which he receives such compensation in the offices of such Member or Resident Commissioner in Washington, District of Columbia, or in the State or the district which such Member or Resident Commissioner represents.

(Pub. L. 89–90, §103, July 27, 1965, 79 Stat. 281.)

Codification

Section is based on section 2 of House Resolution 294, Eighty-eighth Congress, Aug. 14, 1964, as continued by House Resolution 7, Eighty-ninth Congress, Jan. 4, 1965, which was enacted into permanent law by Pub. L. 89–90.
