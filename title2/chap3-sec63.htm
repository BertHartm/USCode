§63. Duties of Doorkeeper of Senate

The Doorkeeper of the Senate shall perform the usual services pertaining to his office during the session of Congress, and shall in the recess, under the direction of the Secretary of the Senate, take care of the apartments occupied by the Senate.

(R.S. §73.)

Codification

R.S. §73 derived from act Apr. 12, 1792, ch. 20, 1 Stat. 252.
