§2013. Management information system

The Secretary shall establish within the Office, within 1 year after October 19, 1984, a computerized management information system, which shall provide information to the Office. Such information shall include—

(1) student enrollment;

(2) curriculum;

(3) staff;

(4) facilities;

(5) community demographics;

(6) student assessment information; and

(7) information on the administrative and program costs attributable to each Bureau program, divided into discreet elements.

(Pub. L. 95–561, title XI, §1133, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title III, §381, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 4010.)
