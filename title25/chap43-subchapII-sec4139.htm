§4139. Repayment

If a recipient uses grant amounts to provide affordable housing under activities under this subchapter and, at any time during the useful life of the housing the housing does not comply with the requirement under section 4135(a)(2) 1 of this title, the Secretary shall reduce future grant payments on behalf of the grant beneficiary by an amount equal to the grant amounts used for such housing (under the authority under section 4161(a)(2) of this title) or require repayment to the Secretary of an amount equal to such grant amounts.

(Pub. L. 104–330, title II, §209, Oct. 26, 1996, 110 Stat. 4035.)

References in Text

Section 4135(a)(2) of this title, referred to in text, was in the original “section 205(2)”, meaning section 205(2) of Pub. L. 104–330, and was translated as reading section 205(a)(2) of Pub. L. 104–330, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1997, except as otherwise expressly provided, see section 107 of Pub. L. 104–330, set out as a note under section 4101 of this title.

1 See References in Text note below.
