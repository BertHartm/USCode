§996. Payments not “other income and resources”

Payments made under this subchapter shall not be held to be “other income and resources”, as that term is used in sections 302(a)(10)(A), 602(a)(7), 1202(a)(8), and 1352(a)(8) of title 42.

(Pub. L. 87–775, §6, Oct. 9, 1962, 76 Stat. 776.)
