§3338. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter, $2,000,000 for fiscal year 1993 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 102–325, title XIII, §1348, July 23, 1992, 106 Stat. 812.)
