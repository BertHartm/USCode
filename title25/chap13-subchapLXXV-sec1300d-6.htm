§1300d–6. Election of group for enrollment

Any person qualifying for enrollment with more than one group shall elect the group with which he shall be enrolled for the purpose of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 92–555, title III, §302, Oct. 25, 1972, 86 Stat. 1170.)
