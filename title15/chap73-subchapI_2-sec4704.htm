§4704. Expiration date

The authorities under this subchapter shall expire on December 31, 1998.

(Pub. L. 100–418, title II, §2125, Aug. 23, 1988, 102 Stat. 1327; Pub. L. 103–236, title V, §510(a), Apr. 30, 1994, 108 Stat. 465.)

Amendments

1994—Pub. L. 103–236 substituted “1998” for “1993”.

Effective Date of 1994 Amendment

Section 510(b) of Pub. L. 103–236 provided that: “The amendment made by this section [amending this section] shall take effect on December 30, 1993.”
