§5309. Reporting

At the time the President's annual budget request for the Department is submitted, the Secretary shall provide to Congress a detailed review of the progress of the research and development activities authorized under this chapter.

(Pub. L. 101–425, §10, Oct. 15, 1990, 104 Stat. 919.)
