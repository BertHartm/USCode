§5411. Construction

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to limit or otherwise affect the authority of any consensus standards organization to establish, modify, or withdraw any standards and specifications under any other law or authority in effect on November 16, 1990.

(Pub. L. 101 592, §12, Nov. 16, 1990, 104 Stat. 2952.)
