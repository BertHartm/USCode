§687j. Extension of benefits to joint ownership companies

The Administration is authorized to extend the benefits of sections 683(c) and 687i of this title to any small business investment company operating under authority of section 681(d) of this title, and which is owned, in whole or in part, by one or more small business investment companies, in accordance with regulations promulgated by the Administration.

(Pub. L. 85–699, title III, §318, as added Pub. L. 92–595, §2(g), Oct. 27, 1972, 86 Stat. 1316.)
