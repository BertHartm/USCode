§902. Homeland defense activities: funds

(a) 1 The Secretary of Defense may provide funds to a Governor to employ National Guard units or members to conduct homeland defense activities that the Secretary, 2 determines to be necessary and appropriate for participation by the National Guard units or members, as the case may be.

(Added Pub. L. 108–375, div. A, title V, §512(a)(1), Oct. 28, 2004, 118 Stat. 1878.)

1 So in original. No subsec. (b) has been enacted.

2 So in original. The comma probably should not appear.
