§957. Definitions

When used in this chapter—

(1) The term “person” includes individuals, partnerships, corporations, and associations;

(2) The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.

(June 24, 1936, ch. 745, §7, 49 Stat. 1899.)
