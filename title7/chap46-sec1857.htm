§1857. Surplus Disposal Administrator; appointment; duties

The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized to appoint an agricultural surplus disposal administrator whose duties shall include responsibility for activities of the Department, including those of the Commodity Credit Corporation, relating to the disposal of surplus agricultural commodities as the Secretary may direct.

(May 28, 1956, ch. 327, title II, §207, 70 Stat. 200; Oct. 11, 1962, Pub. L. 87–793, §607(a)(6), 76 Stat. 849.)

Amendments

1962—Pub. L. 87–793 struck out provisions which authorized payment of salary at a rate not exceeding $15,000 per annum.

Effective Date of 1962 Amendment

Amendment by Pub. L. 87–793 effective on first day of first pay period which begins on or after Oct. 11, 1962, see section 610 of Pub. L. 87–793.
