§2009cc–18. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $50,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2008 through 2012.

(Pub. L. 87–128, title III, §384S, as added Pub. L. 110–234, title VI, §6027(f), May 22, 2008, 122 Stat. 1183, and Pub. L. 110–246, §4(a), title VI, §6027(f), June 18, 2008, 122 Stat. 1664, 1944.)

Codification

Pub. L. 110–234 and Pub. L. 110–246 enacted identical sections. Pub. L. 110–234 was repealed by section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246.

Effective Date

Enactment of this section and repeal of Pub. L. 110–234 by Pub. L. 110–246 effective May 22, 2008, the date of enactment of Pub. L. 110–234, see section 4 of Pub. L. 110–246, set out as a note under section 8701 of this title.
