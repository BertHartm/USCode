§254. Discrimination by warehouseman prohibited

Every warehouseman conducting a warehouse licensed under this chapter shall receive for storage therein, so far as its capacity permits, any agricultural product of the kind customarily stored therein by him which may be tendered to him in a suitable condition for warehousing, in the usual manner in the ordinary and usual course of business, without making any discrimination between persons desiring to avail themselves of warehouse facilities.

(Aug. 11, 1916, ch. 313, pt. C, §13, 39 Stat. 488.)
