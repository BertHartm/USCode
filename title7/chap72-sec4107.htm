§4107. Support services

The Secretary shall provide such staff personnel, clerical assistance, services, materials, and office space as are essential to assist the Board in carrying out its duties.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title X, §1011, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1262.)
