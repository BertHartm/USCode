§1636i. Termination of authority

The authority provided by this subchapter terminates on September 30, 2010.

(Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966, title II, §260, as added Pub. L. 109–296, §1(a), Oct. 5, 2006, 120 Stat. 1464.)
