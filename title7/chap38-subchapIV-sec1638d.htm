§1638d. Applicability

This subchapter shall apply to the retail sale of a covered commodity beginning September 30, 2004.

(Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966, title II, §285, as added Pub. L. 107–171, title X, §10816, May 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 535.)
