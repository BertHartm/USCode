§4604. Notice and hearing

Whenever the Secretary has reason to believe that the issuance of an order will assist in carrying out the purpose of this chapter, the Secretary shall provide due notice of and opportunity for a hearing upon a proposed order. Such hearing may be requested and a proposal for an order submitted by any organization or interested person affected by the provisions of this chapter.

(Pub. L. 98–590, §5, Oct. 30, 1984, 98 Stat. 3117.)
