§4101. Establishment of Board

There is hereby established an advisory board to be known as the National Agricultural Cost of Production Standards Review Board (hereafter in this chapter referred to as the “Board”).

(Pub. L. 97–98, title X, §1005, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1261.)

Effective Date

Chapter effective Dec. 22, 1981, see section 1801 of Pub. L. 97–98, set out as a note under section 4301 of this title.
