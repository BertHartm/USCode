§2009bb–13. Termination of authority

The authority provided by this subchapter terminates effective October 1, 2007.

(Pub. L. 87–128, title III, §383N, as added Pub. L. 107–171, title VI, §6028, May 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 387.)
