§950aaa–5. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter $100,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1996 through 2002.

(Pub. L. 101–624, title XXIII, §2335A, as added Pub. L. 104–127, title VII, §704, Apr. 4, 1996, 110 Stat. 1112.)
