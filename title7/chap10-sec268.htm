§268. Rules and regulations

The Secretary of Agriculture shall from time to time make such rules and regulations as he may deem necessary for the efficient execution of the provisions of this chapter.

(Aug. 11, 1916, ch. 313, pt. C, §28, 39 Stat. 490.)
