§2009aa–13. Termination of authority

This subchapter and the authority provided under this subchapter expire on October 1, 2002.

(Pub. L. 87–128, title III, §382N, as added Pub. L. 106–554, §1(a)(4) [div. B, title V, §503], Dec. 21, 2000, 114 Stat. 2763, 2763A–281.)
