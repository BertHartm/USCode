§5106. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter $7,500,000 for each of the fiscal years 1988 through 2010.

(Pub. L. 100–233, title V, §506, Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1664; Pub. L. 101–624, title XVIII, §1853, Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 3837; Pub. L. 103–354, title II, §282(e), Oct. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 3235; Pub. L. 106–472, title III, §306(c), Nov. 9, 2000, 114 Stat. 2073; Pub. L. 109–17, §1, June 29, 2005, 119 Stat. 339.)

Amendments

2005—Pub. L. 109–17 substituted “2010” for “2005”.

2000—Pub. L. 106–472 substituted “2005” for “2000”.

1994—Pub. L. 103–354 substituted “2000” for “1995”.

1990—Pub. L. 101–624 substituted “1995” for “1991”.
