§950aaa. Purpose

The purposes of this chapter are to provide incentives for local telephone exchange carriers, rural community facilities and rural residents to improve the quality of phone service, to provide access to advanced telecommunications services and computer networks, and to improve rural opportunities.

(Pub. L. 101–624, title XXIII, §2331, Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 4017.)
