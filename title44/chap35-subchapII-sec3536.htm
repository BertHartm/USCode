§3536. Expiration

This subchapter shall not be in effect after the date that is two years after the date on which this subchapter takes effect.

(Added Pub. L. 106–398, §1 [[div. A], title X, §1061], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–272.)

References in Text

The date on which this subchapter takes effect, referred to in text, is 30 days after Oct. 30, 2000, see section 1 [[div. A], title X, §1065] of Pub. L. 106–398, set out as an Effective Date note under section 3531 of this title.
