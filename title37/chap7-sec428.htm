§428. Allowance for recruiting expenses

In addition to other pay or allowances authorized by law, and under uniform regulations prescribed by the Secretaries concerned, a member who is assigned to recruiting duties for his armed force may be reimbursed for actual and necessary expenses incurred in connection with those duties.

(Added Pub. L. 92–129, title II, §205(a), Sept. 28, 1971, 85 Stat. 359.)

Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1971, see section 209 of Pub. L. 92–129, set out as a note under section 302a of this title.

Pay Continuation

Enactment of this section not to reduce the pay to which any member of the uniformed services was entitled on June 30, 1971, see section 210 of Pub. L. 92–129, set out as a note under section 203 of this title.
