§51907. Manufacture and sale of awards and replacements

The Secretary of Transportation may—

(1) authorize private persons to manufacture decorations and medals authorized under this chapter or a prior law; and

(2) provide at cost, or authorize private persons to sell at reasonable prices, replacements for those decorations and medals.

(Pub. L. 109–304, §8(b), Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1585; Pub. L. 109–163, div. C, title XXXV, §3510, Jan. 6, 2006, 119 Stat. 3557.)

Amendment Not Shown in Text

This section was derived from section 2004(c) of the former Appendix to this title, which was amended by Pub. L. 109–163, div. C, title XXXV, §3510, Jan. 6, 2006, 119 Stat. 3557. For applicability of that amendment to this section, see section 18(a) of Pub. L. 109–304, set out as a Legislative Purpose and Construction note preceding section 101 of this title. Section 2004(c) of the former Appendix to this title was amended by striking “provide at cost, or authorize for the manufacture and sale at reasonable prices by private persons—” and inserting “provide—”.
