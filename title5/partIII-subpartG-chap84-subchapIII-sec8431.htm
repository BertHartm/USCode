[§8431. Repealed. Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I, §101(f) [title VI, §659 [title II, §206(a)(2)]], Sept. 30, 1996, 110 Stat. 3009–314, 3009–372, 3009–378]

Section, added Pub. L. 99–335, title I, §101(a), June 6, 1986, 100 Stat. 541; amended Pub. L. 101–509, title V, §529 [title I, §101(b)(6)(B)], Nov. 5, 1990, 104 Stat. 1427, 1440, provided that for purpose of this subchapter, “basic pay”, when used with respect to an employee or Member, meant basic pay established pursuant to law, without regard to any provision of law (except sections 5303(e), 5304(g), and 5382(b) of this title) limiting rate of pay actually payable in any pay period (including any provision of law restricting use of appropriated funds).

Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 30, 1996, see section 101(f) [title VI, §659 [title II, §207]] of Pub. L. 104–208, set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under section 5545a of this title.
