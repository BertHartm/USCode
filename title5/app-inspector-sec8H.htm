§8H. Rule of construction of special provisions

The special provisions under section 8, 8A, 8B, 8C,1 8D,1 or 8E 1 of this Act relate only to the establishment named in such section and no inference shall be drawn from the presence or absence of a provision in any such section with respect to an establishment not named in such section or with respect to a designated Federal entity as defined under section 8F(a).1

(Pub. L. 95–452, §8H, formerly §8F, as added Pub. L. 100–504, title I, §105, Oct. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 2525; renumbered §8G and amended Pub. L. 103–82, title II, §202(g)(1), (5)(B), Sept. 21, 1993, 107 Stat. 889, 890; renumbered §8H, Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I, §101(f) [title VI, §662(b)(3)], Sept. 30, 1996, 110 Stat. 3009–314, 3009–380.)

References in Text

Sections 8C and 8D of this Act, referred to in text, were renumbered sections 8D and 8E, respectively, and a new section 8C enacted by Pub. L. 103–204, §23(a)(2), (3), Dec. 17, 1993, 107 Stat. 2407, 2408.

Section 8E of this Act, referred to in text, which was added by Pub. L. 103–82, title II, §202(g)(1), Sept. 21, 1993, 107 Stat. 889, was renumbered section 8F by Pub. L. 103–204, §23(a)(3), Dec. 17, 1993, 107 Stat. 2408.

Section 8F, referred to in text, which related to requirements for Federal entities and designated Federal entities, was renumbered section 8G by Pub. L. 103–204, §23(a)(3), Dec. 17, 1993, 107 Stat. 2408, and is set out preceding this section.
