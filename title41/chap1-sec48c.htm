§48c. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Committee to carry out sections 46 to 48c of this title $240,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1974, and such sums as may be necessary for the succeeding fiscal years.

(June 25, 1938, ch. 697, §6, as added Pub. L. 92–28, §1, June 23, 1971, 85 Stat. 82; amended Pub. L. 93–76, July 30, 1973, 87 Stat. 176; Pub. L. 93–358, §1(4), July 25, 1974, 88 Stat. 393.)

Amendments

1974—Pub. L. 93–358 substituted “$240,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1974, and such sums as may be necessary for the succeeding fiscal years” for “$200,000 each for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1972, and the next succeeding fiscal year, and $240,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1974”.

1973—Pub. L. 93–76 increased authorization of appropriation to $240,000 for fiscal year ending June 30, 1974.

Effective Date

Section effective Aug. 1, 1971, see section 2 of Pub. L. 92–28, set out as an Effective Date of 1971 Amendment note under section 46 of this title.
