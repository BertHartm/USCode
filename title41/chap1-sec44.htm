§44. Separability of Walsh-Healey provisions

If any provision of sections 35 to 45 of this title, or the application thereof to any persons or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of said sections, and the application of such provisions to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.

(June 30, 1936, ch. 881, §12, formerly §10, 49 Stat. 2039; renumbered §11, June 30, 1952, ch. 530, title III, §301, 66 Stat. 308; renumbered §12, Pub. L. 104–106, div. D, title XLIII, §4321(f)(1)(B), Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 675.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 12 of act June 30, 1936, was renumbered section 13, and is classified to section 45 of this title.

Another prior section 12 of act June 30, 1936, was renumbered section 14, and is set out as a Short Title note under section 35 of this title.
