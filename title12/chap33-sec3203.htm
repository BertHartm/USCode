§3203. Dual service of management official of $1,000,000,000 institution or holding company as management official of unaffiliated $500,000,000 institution or holding company prohibited

If a depository institution or a depository holding company has total assets exceeding $1,000,000,000, a management official of such institution or any affiliate thereof may not serve as a management official of any other nonaffiliated depository institution or depository holding company having total assets exceeding $500,000,000 or as a management official of any affiliate of such other institution.

(Pub. L. 95–630, title II, §204, Nov. 10, 1978, 92 Stat. 3673.)
