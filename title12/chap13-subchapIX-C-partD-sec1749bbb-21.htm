§1749bbb–21. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title XII, §1251, formerly §1241, as added Pub. L. 90–448, title XI, §1103, Aug. 1, 1968, 82 Stat. 566; renumbered §1251, Pub. L. 91–609, title VI, §602(d), Dec. 31, 1970, 84 Stat. 1789.)
