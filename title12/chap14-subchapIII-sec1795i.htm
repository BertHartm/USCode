§1795i. Annual report

The annual report required by section 1752a(e) 1 of this title shall include a full report of the activities of the Facility.

(June 26, 1934, ch. 750, title III, formerly subch. III, §310, as added Nov. 10, 1978, Pub. L. 95–630, title XVIII, §1802, 92 Stat. 3723; amended Mar. 31, 1980, Pub. L. 96–221, title III, §309(b)(1), 94 Stat. 149.)

Codification

Section 309(b)(1) of Pub. L. 96–221 redesignated subch. III as title III of act June 26, 1934, ch. 750, cited as a credit to this section.

1 So in original. Probably should be section “1752a(d)”.
