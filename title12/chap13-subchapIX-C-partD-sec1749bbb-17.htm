§1749bbb–17. General powers of Director

In the performance of, and with respect to, the functions, powers, and duties vested in him by this subchapter, the Director shall (in addition to any authority otherwise vested in him) have the functions, powers, and duties (including the authority to issue rules and regulations) of the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development set forth in section 1749a,1 except subsections (c)(2), (d), and (f), of this title. Any rules or regulations of the Director shall only be issued after full consultation with the Board and after notice and hearing, if granted, as required by subchapter II of chapter 5, and chapter 7, of title 5.

References in Text
