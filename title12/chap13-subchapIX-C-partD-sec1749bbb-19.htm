§1749bbb–19. Advance payments

Any payments which are made under the authority of this subchapter may be made, after necessary adjustments on account of previously made underpayments or overpayments in advance or by way of reimbursement. Payments may be made in such installments and on such conditions as the Director may determine.

(June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title XII, §1249, formerly §1239, as added Pub. L. 90–448, title XI, §1103, Aug. 1, 1968, 82 Stat. 566; renumbered §1249, Pub. L. 91–609, title VI, §602(d), Dec. 31, 1970, 84 Stat. 1789; amended Pub. L. 98–181, title IV, §452(b)(1), Nov. 30, 1983, 97 Stat. 1230.)

Amendments

1983—Pub. L. 98–181 substituted “Director” for “Secretary”.
