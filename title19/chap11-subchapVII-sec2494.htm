§2494. Progress reports

The President shall include as a part of the annual report required under section 2291(e)(1) 1 of title 22 an evaluation of progress that each major drug producing country and each major drug-transit country has made during the reporting period in achieving the objectives set forth in section 2492(b) of this title.

(Pub. L. 93–618, title VIII, §804, as added Pub. L. 99–570, title IX, §9001, Oct. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 3207–166.)

References in Text

Subsec. (e) of section 2291 of title 22, referred to in text, was repealed and subsec. (i) was redesignated (e) by Pub. L. 102–583, §6(b)(2), (3), Nov. 2, 1992, 106 Stat. 4932. For successor provisions to former subsec. (e), see sections 2291h(a) and 2291i(a) of Title 22, Foreign Relations and Intercourse.
