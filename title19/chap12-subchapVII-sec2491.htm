§2491. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Narcotics Control Trade Act”.

(Pub. L. 93–618, title VIII, §801, as added Pub. L. 99–570, title IX, §9001, Oct. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 3207–164.)
