§4812. Reservation of right to amend or repeal chapter

The right to amend, alter, or repeal this chapter is expressly reserved to the Congress.

(Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XVI, §1613, Nov. 5, 1990, 104 Stat. 1738.)
