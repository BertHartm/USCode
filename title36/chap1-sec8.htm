§8. Reservation of right to amend or repeal certain sections

Congress shall have the right to repeal, alter, or amend sections 1, 2 to 6, 8, and 9 of this title at any time.

(Jan. 5, 1905, ch. 23, §7, 33 Stat. 602.)
