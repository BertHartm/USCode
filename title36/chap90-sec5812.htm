§5812. Reservation of right to alter, amend, or repeal chapter

The right to alter, amend, or repeal this chapter is expressly reserved to Congress.

(Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title XV, §1513, Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1966.)
