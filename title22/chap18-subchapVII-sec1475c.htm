§1475c. Acting Associate Directors

If an Associate Director of the United States Information Agency dies, resigns, or is sick or absent, the Associate Director's principal assistant shall perform the duties of the office until a successor is appointed or the absence or sickness stops.

(Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36, title VIII, §808, as added Aug. 24, 1982, Pub. L. 97–241, title III, §304(e), 96 Stat. 293.)
