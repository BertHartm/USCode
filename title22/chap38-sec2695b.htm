§2695b. Cost of presence of departments and agencies outside United States

In fiscal year 1998, a system shall be in place that allocates to each department and agency the full cost of its presence outside of the United States.

(Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I, §101(a) [title IV], Sept. 30, 1996, 110 Stat. 3009, 3009–46.)
