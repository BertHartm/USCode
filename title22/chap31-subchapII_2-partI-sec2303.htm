§2303. Excess defense articles

Excess defense articles shall be provided whenever possible rather than providing such articles by the procurement of new items.

(Pub. L. 87–195, pt. II, §502A, as added Pub. L. 93–189, §12(a), Dec. 17, 1973, 87 Stat. 720.)
