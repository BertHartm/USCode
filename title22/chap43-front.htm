Repeal of Chapter

Chapter repealed effective Sept. 30, 1995, or the date on which all members of the Broadcasting Board of Governors within the U.S. Information Agency established under section 6203 of this title are confirmed, whichever is earlier, see section 6209(e) of this title.

Chapter Referred to in Other Sections

This chapter is referred to in sections 6207, 6209 of this title.
