§2349bb–3. Limitations

The limitations contained in section 2349aa–2(a) and (d) of this title shall apply to this part.

(Pub. L. 87–195, pt. II, §584, as added Pub. L. 106–280, title III, §301, Oct. 6, 2000, 114 Stat. 852.)
