§277d–46. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated a total of $156,000,000 for fiscal years 2001 through 2005 to carry out sections 277d–43 to 277d–46 of this title. Such sums shall remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 106–457, title VIII, §806, Nov. 7, 2000, 114 Stat. 1981.)
