§6553. Abolition of United States Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy

(a) Abolition

The United States Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy is abolished.

(b) Omitted

(Pub. L. 105–277, div. G, subdiv. A, title XIII, §1334, Oct. 21, 1998, 112 Stat. 2681–786.)

Codification

Section is comprised of section 1334 of Pub. L. 105–277. Subsec. (b) of section 1334 of Pub. L. 105–277 repealed section 1469 of this title and amended Reorganization Plan No. 2 of 1977, set out as a note under section 1461 of this title and in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
