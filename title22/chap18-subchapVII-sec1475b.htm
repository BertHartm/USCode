§1475b. Seal of United States Information Agency; judicial notice

The seal of the United States Information Agency shall be the arms and crest of the United States, encircled by the words “United States Information Agency”. Judicial notice shall be taken of the seal.

(Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36, title VIII, §807, as added Oct. 7, 1978, Pub. L. 95–426, title II, §204(c), 92 Stat. 974; amended Aug. 24, 1982, Pub. L. 97–241, title III, §303(b), 96 Stat. 291.)

Transfer of Functions

“United States Information Agency” substituted in text for “International Communication Agency” pursuant to section 303(b) of Pub. L. 97–241, set out as a note under section 1461 of this title.
