§290n. Establishment of Border Health Commission

The President is authorized and encouraged to conclude an agreement with Mexico to establish a binational commission to be known as the United States-Mexico Border Health Commission.

(Pub. L. 103–400, §2, Oct. 22, 1994, 108 Stat. 4169.)

Short Title

Section 1 of Pub. L. 103–400 provided that: “This Act [enacting this subchapter] may be cited as the ‘United States-Mexico Border Health Commission Act’.”
