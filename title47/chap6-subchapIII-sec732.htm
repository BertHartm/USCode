§732. Applicable laws

The corporation shall be subject to the provisions of this chapter and, to the extent consistent with this chapter, to the District of Columbia Business Corporation Act [D.C. Code, §29–301 et seq.]. The right to repeal, alter, or amend this chapter at any time is expressly reserved.

(Pub. L. 87–624, title III, §302, as added Pub. L. 103–414, title III, §304(b)(2), Oct. 25, 1994, 108 Stat. 4297.)

References in Text

The District of Columbia Business Corporation Act, referred to in text, is act June 8, 1954, ch. 269, 68 Stat. 179, as amended, which appears in chapter 3 (§29–301 et seq.) of Title 29, Corporations, of the District of Columbia Code.

Prior Provisions

A prior section 732, Pub. L. 87–624, title III, §302, Aug. 31, 1962, 76 Stat. 423, related to process of organization of the communications satellite corporation, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 103–414, §304(b)(2).
