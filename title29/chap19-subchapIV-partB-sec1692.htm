§1692. Establishment of the Job Corps

There shall be within the Department of Labor a “Job Corps”.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, §422, Oct. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1370.)

Repeal of Section

Pub. L. 105–220, title I, §199(b)(2), (c)(2)(B), Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1059, provided that this section is repealed effective July 1, 2000.
