§2288. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $8,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1995 through 1998.

(b) Availability in succeeding fiscal year

Amounts appropriated under subsection (a) of this section shall remain available for obligation for the fiscal year immediately following the fiscal year for which such amounts were appropriated.

(c) Reservation

Of the amounts appropriated under subsection (a) of this section, the Secretary shall reserve $250,000 for the purpose of providing information and technical assistance to States under section 2286 of this title.

(Pub. L. 100–407, title III, §308, as added Pub. L. 103–218, title III, §301, Mar. 9, 1994, 108 Stat. 95.)
