§795o. Restriction

Each State agency designated under section 795n(b)(1) of this title shall collect the client information required by section 712 of this title separately for supported employment clients under this part and for supported employment clients under subchapter I of this chapter.

(Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, §636, as added Pub. L. 102–569, title VI, §621(a), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4442.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 795o, Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, §636, as added Pub. L. 99–506, title VII, §704(a)(1), Oct. 21, 1986, 100 Stat. 1836, contained provisions similar to this section, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 102–569, §621(a).
