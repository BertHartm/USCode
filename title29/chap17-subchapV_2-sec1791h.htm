§1791h. Implementing regulations

The Secretary shall promulgate regulations implementing this subchapter not later than January 31, 1993.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title V, §509, as added Pub. L. 100–628, title VII, §712(a)(3), Nov. 7, 1988, 102 Stat. 3253; amended Pub. L. 102–367, title V, §501, Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1099.)

Amendments

1992—Pub. L. 102–367 amended section generally, substituting provisions relating to implementing regulations for provisions relating to information and data collection.

Effective Date of 1992 Amendment

Amendment by Pub. L. 102–367 effective July 1, 1993, see section 701(a) of Pub. L. 102–367, set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment; Transition Provisions note under section 1501 of this title.
