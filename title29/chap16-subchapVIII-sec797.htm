§797. Authorization of appropriations

(a) Demonstration projects

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 797a of this title, such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1997.

(b) Training initiatives

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out section 797b of this title, such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1993 through 1997.

(Pub. L. 93–112, title VIII, §801, as added Pub. L. 102–569, title VIII, §801(a), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4469.)
