§3037. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter, and the provisions of section 763 of this title that relate to research described in section 763(b)(2)(A) of this title, $10,000,000 for fiscal year 1999, and such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2000.

(Pub. L. 105–394, title II, §217, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3657.)

References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title II of Pub. L. 105–394, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3651, which enacted this subchapter and amended sections 763, 781, 792, and 794b of this title and section 3710 of Title 15, Commerce and Trade. For complete classification of title II to the Code, see Tables.
