§1646. Transfer of funds

A service delivery area may transfer up to 10 percent of the amounts allocated to the service delivery area under section 1642(b) of this title to the program under part A of this subchapter if such transfer is—

(1) described in the job training plan; and

(2) approved by the Governor.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title II, §266, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title II, §208, Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1073.)

Effective Date

Section effective July 1, 1993, see section 701(a) of Pub. L. 102–367, set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment; Transition Provisions note under section 1501 of this title.
