§1709. Donations

The Secretary is authorized to accept on behalf of the Job Corps or individual Job Corps centers charitable donations of cash or other assistance, including but not limited to, equipment and materials, if such donations are available for appropriate use for the purposes set forth in this part.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, §439, Oct. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1380.)
