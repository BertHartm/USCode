§1782f. Reporting

The Secretary is authorized to establish such reporting procedures as are necessary to carry out the purposes of this part.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, §497, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, §406, Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1091.)
