§795a. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this part, such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 1999 through 2003.

(Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, §612, as added Pub. L. 105–220, title IV, §409, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1214.)

Provisions similar to this section were contained in section 795i of this title prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 105–220.
