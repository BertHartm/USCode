§1771. Statement of purpose

The purpose of this part is to establish a National Commission for Employment Policy which shall have the responsibility for examining broad issues of development, coordination, and administration of employment and training programs, and for advising the President and the Congress on national employment and training issues. For the purpose of providing funds for the Commission, the Secretary shall reserve $2,000,000 of the sums appropriated for this subchapter for each fiscal year.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, §471, Oct. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1387.)
