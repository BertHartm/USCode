§1784b. Definitions

As used in this part, the term “unit of general local government” includes—

(1) in the case of a community conducting a project in an Indian reservation or Alaska Native village, the grantee designated under subsection (c) or (d) of section 1671 of this title, or a consortium of such grantees and the State; and

(2) in the case of a community conducting a project in a migrant or seasonal farmworker community, the grantee designated under section 1672(c) of this title, or a consortium of such grantees and the State.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, §499C, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, §408, Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1095.)
