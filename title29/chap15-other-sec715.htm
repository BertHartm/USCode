§715. Transfer of funds

(a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, no funds appropriated under this chapter for any research program or activity may be used for any purpose other than that for which the funds were specifically authorized.

(b) No more than 1 percent of funds appropriated for discretionary grants, contracts, or cooperative agreements authorized by this chapter may be used for the purpose of providing non-Federal panels of experts to review applications for such grants, contracts, or cooperative agreements.

(Pub. L. 93–112, §16, as added Pub. L. 95–602, title I, §122(a)(10), Nov. 6, 1978, 92 Stat. 2987; amended Pub. L. 99–506, title I, §107, Oct. 21, 1986, 100 Stat. 1812; Pub. L. 102–569, title I, §108(a), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4363.)

Amendments

1992—Subsec. (b). Pub. L. 102–569 substituted “1 percent” for “one-half of 1 percent”.

1986—Pub. L. 99–506 substituted “Transfer of funds” for “Use of funds” in section catchline and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “No funds appropriated under this chapter for any research program or activity may be used for any purpose other than that for which the funds were specifically authorized.”
