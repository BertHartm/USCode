§763. Transferred

Codification

Section, Pub. L. 93–112, title II, §203, Sept. 26, 1973, 87 Stat. 376, relating to making of grants and contracts for training of personnel involved in vocational services to handicapped individuals, was redesignated as section 304 of Pub. L. 93–112 and transferred to section 774 of this title.
