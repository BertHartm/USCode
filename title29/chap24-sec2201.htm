§§2201, 2202. Repealed. Pub. L. 105–394, title IV, §401, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3661

Section 2201, Pub. L. 100–407, §2, Aug. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 1044; Pub. L. 103–218, §3, Mar. 9, 1994, 108 Stat. 51, related to findings, purposes, and policy.

Section 2202, Pub. L. 100–407, §3, Aug. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 1046; Pub. L. 103–218, §4, Mar. 9, 1994, 108 Stat. 54; Pub. L. 105–244, title I, §102(a)(9)(B), Oct. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1620, related to definitions.

Short Title

Pub. L. 100–407, §1, Aug. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 1044, provided that Pub. L. 100–407 could be cited as the “Technology-Related Assistance for Individuals With Disabilities Act of 1988”, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 105–394, title IV, §401, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3661.
