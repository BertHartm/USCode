§1537. Interstate agreements

In the event that compliance with provisions of this chapter would be enhanced by cooperative agreements between States, the consent of Congress is hereby given to such States to enter into such compacts and agreements to facilitate such compliance, subject to the approval of the Secretary.

(Pub. L. 97–300, title I, §127, Oct. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1343.)
