§795q. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 1993 through 1997.

(Pub. L. 93–112, title VI, §638, as added Pub. L. 102–569, title VI, §621(a), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4442.)

L. 100–630, title II, §207(h), Nov. 7, 1988, 102 Stat. 3314; Pub.
