§5991. Purposes

The purposes of this subchapter are—

(1) to develop a national park passport that includes a collectible stamp to be used for admission to units of the National Park System; and

(2) to generate revenue for support of the National Park System.

(Pub. L. 105–391, title VI, §601, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3518.)

Repeal of Section

Section repealed on the date the notice required by section 6804(a)(3) of this title is published in the Federal Register, see section 6812(d)(2) of this title.
