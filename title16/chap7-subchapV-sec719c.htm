§719c. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for administrative expenses of the Program $250,000 for each of the fiscal years 2001 through 2005.

(Pub. L. 103–340, §6, formerly §5, Oct. 6, 1994, 108 Stat. 3120; renumbered §6 and amended Pub. L. 106–316, §§1, 2(2), Oct. 19, 2000, 114 Stat. 1276.)

Codification

Another section 6 of Pub. L. 103–340 enacted provisions listed in a table of National Wildlife Refuges set out under section 668dd of this title.

Amendments

2000—Pub. L. 106–316, §1, substituted “for each of the fiscal years 2001 through 2005” for “for each of the fiscal years 1995 through 2000”.
