§5306. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Fund $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, and 2000 to carry out this chapter, to remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 103–391, §7, Oct. 22, 1994, 108 Stat. 4097.)
