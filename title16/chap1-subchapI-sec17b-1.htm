§17b–1. Repealed. Pub. L. 91–383, §12(c), as added Pub. L. 94–458, §2, Oct. 7, 1976, 90 Stat. 1943

Section, acts July 31, 1953, ch. 298, title I, §1, 67 Stat. 271; July 14, 1956, ch. 598, 70 Stat. 543, related to transmittal of reports of proposed contract and lease awards to Congressional officers by Secretary. See section 1a–7(c) of this title.

The last sentence of section 12(c) of Pub. L. 91–383, as added by Pub. L. 94–458, repealed this section. The remainder of section 12(c) enacted section 1a–7(c) of this title.
