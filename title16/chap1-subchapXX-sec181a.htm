§181a. Administrative control of Blackfeet Highway and connections; jurisdiction of Secretary of the Interior

The Secretary of the Interior shall notify, in writing, the Governor of the State of Montana of the passage and approval of this section and section 181 of this title, and so far as the interests of the United States shall require, the said Secretary shall exercise administrative control and jurisdiction over said rights of way and highways through the National Park Service.

(May 2, 1932, ch. 155, §2, 47 Stat. 144.)
