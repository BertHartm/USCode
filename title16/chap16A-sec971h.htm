§971h. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter, including use for payment of the United States share of the joint expenses of the Commission as provided in Article X of the Convention, the following sums:

(1) For fiscal year 1995, $4,103,000, of which $50,000 are authorized in the aggregate for the advisory committee established under section 971b of this title and the species working groups established under section 971b–1 of this title, and $2,890,000 are authorized for research activities under this chapter and the Act of September 4, 1980 (16 U.S.C. 971i).

(2) For fiscal year 1996, $5,453,000, of which $50,000 are authorized in the aggregate for such advisory committee and such working groups, and $4,240,000 are authorized for such research activities.

(3) For fiscal year 1997, $5,465,000 of which $62,000 are authorized in the aggregate for such advisory committee and such working groups, and $4,240,000 are authorized for such research activities.

(4) For each of fiscal years 1998, 1999, 2000, and 2001, $5,465,000 of which $75,000 are authorized in the aggregate for such advisory committee and such working groups, and $4,240,000 are authorized for such research activities.
