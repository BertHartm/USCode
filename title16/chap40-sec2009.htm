§2009. Termination of program

The provisions of this chapter shall terminate on December 31, 2008.

(Pub. L. 95–192, §10, Nov. 18, 1977, 91 Stat. 1411; Pub. L. 99–198, title XII, §1252(d), Dec. 23, 1985, 99 Stat. 1517.)

Amendments

1985—Pub. L. 99–198 substituted “2008” for “1985”.
