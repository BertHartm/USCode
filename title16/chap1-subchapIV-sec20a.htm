§20a. Authority of Secretary of the Interior to encourage concessioners

Subject to the findings and policy stated in section 20 of this title, the Secretary of the Interior shall take such action as may be appropriate to encourage and enable private persons and corporations (hereinafter referred to as “concessioners”) to provide and operate facilities and services which he deems desirable for the accommodation of visitors in areas administered by the National Park Service.

(Pub. L. 89–249, §2, Oct. 9, 1965, 79 Stat. 969.)
