§778b. Cooperation with Department of Agriculture

The Department of Agriculture is authorized to cooperate in carrying out the provisions of this chapter by furnishing such information and assistance as may be requested by the Secretary of the Interior.

(Pub. L. 85–342, §3, Mar. 15, 1958, 72 Stat. 35.)
