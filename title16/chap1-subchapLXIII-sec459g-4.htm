§459g–4. Administration; public outdoor recreation and enjoyment; utilization of authorities for conservation and development of natural resources

The Secretary shall administer the Cape Lookout National Seashore for the general purposes of public outdoor recreation, including conservation of natural features contributing to public enjoyment. In the administration of the seashore and the administrative site, the Secretary may utilize such statutory authorities relating to areas administered and supervised by the Secretary through the National Park Service and such statutory authorities otherwise available to him for the conservation and management of natural resources as he deems appropriate to carry out the purposes of sections 459g to 459g–7 of this title.

(Pub. L. 89–366, §5, Mar. 10, 1966, 80 Stat. 35.)
