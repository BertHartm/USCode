§3443. Approval of plans

The Secretary shall submit each plan developed under section 3442 of this title to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry of the Senate and the Committee on Agriculture of the House of Representatives. The Secretary may implement any such plan only after each such committee adopts a resolution approving the plan.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, §1523, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1336.)
