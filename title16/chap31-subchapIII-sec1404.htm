§1404. Reports

The Commission shall transmit to Congress, by January 31 of each year, a report which shall include—

(1) a description of the activities and accomplishments of the Commission during the immediately preceding year; and

(2) all the findings and recommendations made by and to the Commission pursuant to section 1402 of this title together with the responses made to these recommendations.

(Pub. L. 92–522, title II, §204, Oct. 21, 1972, 86 Stat. 1045.)
