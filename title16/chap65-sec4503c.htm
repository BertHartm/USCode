§4503c. Annual report on Institutes of Tropical Forestry

The Secretary shall make annual reports to Congress on the progress, needs, and long-range plans of the Institutes of Tropical Forestry in meeting the requirements of section 6706 of title 7. Such reports shall be submitted by the Secretary pursuant to section 1606(c) of this title.

(Pub. L. 101–513, title VI, §607, as added Pub. L. 102–574, §2(a)(2), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4595.)

Prior Provisions

A prior section 607 of Pub. L. 101–513, which amended sections 1641, 1643, 2101, and 2109 of this title, was renumbered section 611.
