§3418. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated annually, to be available until expended, such sums as may be necessary to carry out the program authorized by this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, §1509, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1332.)
