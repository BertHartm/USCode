§3444. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated, for each of the fiscal years 1983 through 1987, such sums as may be necessary for carrying out the provisions of this subchapter, such sums to remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, §1524, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1336.)
