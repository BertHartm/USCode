§403c. Use of existing commission

The Secretary of the Interior may for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of sections 403 and 403a to 403c of this title employ the commission authorized by the Act approved February 21, 1925.

(May 22, 1926, ch. 363, §4, 44 Stat. 617.)

References in Text

Act February 21, 1925, ch. 281, 43 Stat. 958, referred to in text, was a temporary act and was not classified to the Code.
