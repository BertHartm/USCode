§2466. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated—

(1) to the Under Secretary not more than $1,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1991 and 1992 to carry out the purposes of this chapter; and

(2) to the Secretary of State not more than $500,000 for each of fiscal years 1991 and 1992 to carry out section 2464 of this title.

(Pub. L. 101–594, §7, Nov. 16, 1990, 104 Stat. 2978.)
