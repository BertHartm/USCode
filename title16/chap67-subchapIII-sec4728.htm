§4728. Brown tree snake control program

The Task Force shall, within the program developed under subsection (a),1 undertake a comprehensive, environmentally sound program in coordination with regional, territorial, State and local entities to control the brown tree snake (Boiga irregularis) in Guam and other areas where the species is established outside of its historic range.

(Pub. L. 101–646, title I, §1209, Nov. 29, 1990, 104 Stat. 4772; Pub. L. 104–332, §2(h)(1), Oct. 26, 1996, 110 Stat. 4091.)

Amendments

1996—Pub. L. 104–332 made technical amendment to Pub. L. 101–646, §1209, which enacted this section.
