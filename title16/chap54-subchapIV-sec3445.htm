§3445. Reporting requirement

The Secretary shall submit a report evaluating the program authorized under this subchapter to Congress by January 1, 1987. The report shall include a recommendation as to whether the program should be extended and, if so, how it could be strengthened.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, §1525, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1337.)
