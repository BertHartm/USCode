§5108. Authorization of appropriations

To carry out the provisions of this chapter, there are authorized to be appropriated $3,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1995, and $7,000,000 for fiscal year 1996.

(Pub. L. 103–206, title VIII, §809, Dec. 20, 1993, 107 Stat. 2453.)
