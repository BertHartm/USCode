Codification

Section, act Feb. 18, 1929, ch. 257, §3, 45 Stat. 1223, which required the commission created by section 715a of this title, through its chairman, to annually report in detail to Congress on operations of the commission, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66, as amended, set out as a note under section 1113 of Title 31, Money and Finance. See, also, page 177 of House Document No. 103–7.
