§590j. “Agricultural commodity” defined

The term “agricultural commodity” as used in this chapter means any such commodity and any regional or market classification, type, or grade thereof.

(Apr. 27, 1935, ch. 85, §10, as added Feb. 29, 1936, ch. 104, §1, 49 Stat. 1150.)
