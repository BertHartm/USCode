§5962. Use of nonmonetary consideration in concessions contracts

Section 303b of title 40, relating to the leasing of buildings and properties of the United States, shall not apply to contracts awarded by the Secretary pursuant to this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 105–391, title IV, §413, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3515.)
