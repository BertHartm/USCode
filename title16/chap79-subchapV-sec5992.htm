§5992. National park passport program

(a) Program

The Secretary shall establish a national park passport program. A national park passport shall include a collectible stamp providing the holder admission to all units of the National Park System.

(b) Effective period

A national park passport stamp shall be effective for a period of 12 months from the date of purchase.

(c) Transferability

A national park passport and stamp shall not be transferable.

(Pub. L. 105–391, title VI, §602, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3519.)
